Verse_Ch: 29:21 次日，他们向耶和华献平安祭和燔祭，就是献公牛一千只，公绵羊一千只，羊羔一千只，并同献的奠祭；又为以色列众人献许多的祭。 ###EN### And they offered sacrifices to Jehovah and offered up burnt offerings to Jehovah on the morning after that day-one thousand bulls, one thousand rams, one thousand lambs, with their drink offerings and sacrifices in abundance for all Israel.
Verse_Ch: 29:22 那日，他们在耶和华面前吃喝，大大欢乐。他们再次立大卫的儿子所罗门作王，膏他为耶和华作领袖，又膏撒督作祭司。 ###EN### And they ate and drank before Jehovah on that day with great joy. And they made Solomon the son of David king a second time and anointed him as ruler for Jehovah and Zadok as priest.
Verse_Ch: 29:23 于是所罗门坐在耶和华所赐的位上，接续他父亲大卫作王，万事亨通；以色列众人也都听从他。 ###EN### And Solomon sat upon the throne of Jehovah as king in place of David his father and prospered, and all Israel obeyed him.
Verse_Ch: 29:24 众首领和勇士，并大卫王的众子，都服从所罗门王。 ###EN### And all the leaders and mighty men and also all the sons of King David submitted themselves to Solomon the king.
Verse_Ch: 29:25 耶和华使所罗门在以色列众人眼前甚为尊大，又赐他君王的威严，胜过在他以前所有的以色列王。 ###EN### And Jehovah magnified Solomon exceedingly in the sight of all Israel and bestowed upon him a royal majesty that had never been on any king before him over Israel.
Verse_Ch: 29:26 耶西的儿子大卫作全以色列的王。 ###EN### Thus David the son of Jesse reigned over all Israel.
Verse_Ch: 29:27 他作以色列王四十年：在希伯仑作王七年，在耶路撒冷作王三十三年。 ###EN### And the time which he reigned over Israel was forty years; in Hebron he reigned seven years, and in Jerusalem he reigned thirty-three.
Verse_Ch: 29:28 他年纪老迈，日子满足，满享丰富、尊荣，就死了；他儿子所罗门接续他作王。 ###EN### And David died at a good old age, full of days, riches, and honor; and Solomon his son reigned in his place.
Verse_Ch: 29:29 大卫王始终的事，都写在先见撒母耳的书上，和申言者拿单并先见迦得的书上； ###EN### And the acts of David the king, the first and the last, are there written in the chronicles of Samuel the seer and in the chronicles of Nathan the prophet and in the chronicles of Gad the seer,
Verse_Ch: 29:30 他掌王权的事和他的勇力，以及他和以色列并地上列国所经过的事，也都写在这些书上。 ###EN### With all his reign and his might and the times that befell him and Israel and all the kingdoms of the lands.
