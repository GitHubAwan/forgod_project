Verse_Ch: 1:20 王所降的旨意传遍通国，(国度本来广大，)所有的妇人，无论丈夫尊大或卑小，都必尊敬他。 ###EN### And when the king's decree which he will make is heard throughout all his kingdom, vast though it is, all women will give honor to their husbands, great and small.
Verse_Ch: 1:21 王和众首领都以米母干的话为美，王就照这话去行， ###EN### And this word pleased the king and the princes, and the king did according to Memucan's word.
Verse_Ch: 1:22 发诏书到王的各省，依照各省的文字达于各省，依照各族的方言达于各族，使作丈夫的在自己家中作主，各说本族的方言。 ###EN### So he sent letters to all the king's provinces, to each province in its own script and to each people in their own language, that every man should be master in his own house and speak in the language of his own people.

Verse_Ch: 2:1 这些事以后，亚哈随鲁王的忿怒止息，就想念瓦实提和她所行的，并怎样降旨办她。 ###EN### After these things, when the anger of King Ahasuerus subsided, he remembered Vashti and what she had done and what had been decreed against her.

Verse_Ch: 2:13 乃是这样进去见王：从女院到王宫的时候，凡她所要的，都必给她带去。 ###EN### The young woman would go in to the king in this way: Anything that she desired was given her to go with her from the house of the women to the house of the king.

Verse_Ch: 2:22 这事给末底改知道了，他就告诉王后以斯帖；以斯帖奉末底改的名，报告于王。这事经过究察，果然是实，就把二人挂在木架上； ###EN### And the thing became known to Mordecai, and he told Esther the queen; and Esther told the king in Mordecai's name.
Verse_Ch: 2:23 这事且在王面前写在年代志上。 ###EN### And when the matter was investigated and found to be so, the two of them were hanged on the gallows; and it was written in the book of the chronicles in the presence of the king.

Verse_Ch: 3:9 王若以为美，请下诏灭绝他们；我就称一万他连得银子，交给办理王事的人，纳入王的府库。 ###EN### If it please the king, let it be decreed in writing that they be destroyed, and I will pay ten thousand talents of silver into the hands of those who have charge of the king's affairs, that they may bring it into the king's treasuries.
Verse_Ch: 3:10 于是王从自己手上摘下戒指，给犹大人的仇敌亚甲族哈米大他的儿子哈曼。 ###EN### Then the king took his signet ring from his hand and gave it to Haman the son of Hammedatha the Agagite, the Jews' enemy.
Verse_Ch: 3:11 王对哈曼说，这银子仍赐给你，这民也交给你，你看怎样好，就怎样待他们。 ###EN### And the king said to Haman, The silver has been given to you, as well as the people, to do with them as it seems good to you.

Verse_Ch: 3:13 诏书由驿卒送达王的各省，吩咐要将所有的犹大人，无论老少妇女孩子，在一日之间，在十二月，就是亚达月十三日，全然歼毁，杀戮灭绝，并夺他 们的财物为掠物。 ###EN### And letters were sent by couriers to all the king's provinces to destroy, to slay, and to annihilate all the Jews, both young and old, children and women, in one day, that is, the thirteenth day of the twelfth month, which is the month Adar, and to plunder their spoil.
Verse_Ch: 3:14 这文书经过抄录，颁行各省成为法令，向各族宣示，使他们预备等候那日。 ###EN### A copy of the written decree to be issued as law in all the provinces was published to all the peoples so that they would be ready for that day.
Verse_Ch: 3:15 驿卒奉王的谕旨急忙出发；法令也在书珊京城颁布。王和哈曼坐下饮酒，书珊城却一片慌乱。 ###EN### The couriers went out, driven in haste by the king's command; and the decree was issued in Susa the capital. And the king and Haman sat down to drink, but the city of Susa was in confusion.
