Chapter_Header: 1
Verse_Ch: 1:1 耶稣基督的启示，就是神赐给祂，叫祂将必要快发生的事指示祂的众奴仆；祂就借着祂的使者传达，用表号指示祂的奴仆约翰。 ###EN### The revelation of Jesus Christ which God gave to Him to show to His slaves the things that must quickly take place; and He made it known by signs, sending it by His angel to His slave John,
Verse_Ch: 1:2 约翰便将神的话，和耶稣基督的见证，凡自己所看见的，都见证出来。 ###EN### Who testified the word of God and the testimony of Jesus Christ, even all that he saw.
Verse_Ch: 1:3 念这预言的话，和那些听见又遵守其中所记载的，都是有福的，因为时候近了。 ###EN### Blessed is he who reads and those who hear the words of the prophecy and keep the things written in it, for the time is near.
Verse_Ch: 1:4 约翰写信给在亚西亚的七个召会：愿恩典与平安，从那今是昔是以后永是的，从祂宝座前的七灵， ###EN### John to the seven churches which are in Asia: Grace to you and peace from Him who is and who was and who is coming, and from the seven Spirits who are before His throne,
Verse_Ch: 1:5 并从那忠信的见证人、死人中的首生者、为地上君王元首的耶稣基督，归与你们。祂爱我们，用自己的血，把我们从我们的罪中释放了； ###EN### And from Jesus Christ, the faithful Witness, the Firstborn of the dead, and the Ruler of the kings of the earth. To Him who loves us and has released us from our sins by His blood
Verse_Ch: 1:6 又使我们成为国度，作祂神与父的祭司；愿荣耀权能归与祂，直到永永远远。阿们。 ###EN### And made us a kingdom, priests to His God and Father, to Him be the glory and the might forever and ever. Amen.
Verse_Ch: 1:7 看哪，祂驾云降临，众目要看见祂，连刺祂的人也要看见祂；地上的众支派都要因祂捶胸哀哭。是的，阿们。 ###EN### Behold, He comes with the clouds, and every eye will see Him, even those who pierced Him; and all the tribes of the land will mourn over Him. Yes, amen.
Verse_Ch: 1:8 主神说，我是阿拉法，我是俄梅嘎，是今是昔是以后永是的全能者。 ###EN### I am the Alpha and the Omega, says the Lord God, He who is and who was and who is coming, the Almighty.
Verse_Ch: 1:9 我约翰，就是你们的弟兄，和你们在耶稣的患难、国度、忍耐里一同有分的，为神的话和耶稣的见证，曾在那名叫拔摩的海岛上。 ###EN### I John, your brother and fellow partaker in the tribulation and kingdom and endurance in Jesus, was on the island called Patmos because of the word of God and the testimony of Jesus.
Verse_Ch: 1:10 当主日我在灵里，听见在我后面有大声音如吹号说， ###EN### I was in spirit on the Lord's Day and heard behind me a loud voice like a trumpet,
Verse_Ch: 1:11 你所看见的，当写在书上，寄给那七个召会：给以弗所、给士每拿、给别迦摩、给推雅推喇、给撒狄、给非拉铁非、给老底嘉。 ###EN### Saying, What you see write in a scroll and send it to the seven churches: to Ephesus and to Smyrna and to Pergamos and to Thyatira and to Sardis and to Philadelphia and to Laodicea.
Verse_Ch: 1:12 我转过身来，要看是谁发声与我说话；既转过来，就看见七个金灯台； ###EN### And I turned to see the voice that spoke with me; and when I turned, I saw seven golden lampstands,
Verse_Ch: 1:13 灯台中间，有一位好像人子，身穿长袍，直垂到脚，胸间束着金带。 ###EN### And in the midst of the lampstands One like the Son of Man, clothed with a garment reaching to the feet, and girded about at the breasts with a golden girdle.
Verse_Ch: 1:14 祂的头与发皆白，如白羊毛、如雪，眼目如同火焰， ###EN### And His head and hair were as white as white wool, as snow; and His eyes were like a flame of fire;
Verse_Ch: 1:15 脚好像在炉中锻炼过明亮的铜，声音如同众水的声音。 ###EN### And His feet were like shining bronze, as having been fired in a furnace; and His voice was like the sound of many waters.
Verse_Ch: 1:16 祂右手中拿着七星，从祂口中出来一把两刃的利剑，面貌如同烈日中天发光。 ###EN### And He had in His right hand seven stars; and out of His mouth proceeded a sharp two-edged sword; and His face shone as the sun shines in its power.
Verse_Ch: 1:17 我一看见，就仆倒在祂脚前，像死了一样。祂用右手按着我说，不要惧怕；我是首先的，我是末后的， ###EN### And when I saw Him, I fell at His feet as dead; and He placed His right hand on me, saying, Do not fear; I am the First and the Last
Verse_Ch: 1:18 又是那活着的；我曾死过，看哪，现在又活了，直活到永永远远，并且拿着死亡和阴间的钥匙。 ###EN### And the living One; and I became dead, and behold, I am living forever and ever; and I have the keys of death and of Hades.
Verse_Ch: 1:19 所以你要把所看见的事，和现在的事，以及这些事以后将要发生的事，都写出来。 ###EN### Write therefore the things which you have seen and the things which are and the things which are about to take place after these things.
Verse_Ch: 1:20 论到你所看见在我右手中的七星，和七个金灯台的奥秘，那七星就是七个召会的使者，七灯台就是七个召会。 ###EN### The mystery of the seven stars which you saw upon My right hand and the seven golden lampstands: The seven stars are the messengers of the seven churches, and the seven lampstands are the seven churches.
Chapter_Header: 2
Verse_Ch: 2:1 你要写信给在以弗所的召会的使者，说，那右手中握着七星，在七个金灯台中间行走的，这样说， ###EN### To the messenger of the church in Ephesus write: These things says He who holds the seven stars in His right hand, He who walks in the midst of the seven golden lampstands:
Verse_Ch: 2:2 我知道你的行为、劳碌、忍耐，也知道你不能容忍恶人；你也曾试验那自称是使徒却不是使徒的，看出他们是假的； ###EN### I know your works and your labor and your endurance and that you cannot bear evil men; and you have tried those who call themselves apostles and are not, and have found them to be false;
Verse_Ch: 2:3 你也有忍耐，曾为我的名忍受一切，并不乏倦。 ###EN### And you have endurance and have borne all things because of My name and have not grown weary.
Verse_Ch: 2:4 然而有一件事我要责备你，就是你离弃了起初的爱。 ###EN### But I have one thing against you, that you have left your first love.
Verse_Ch: 2:5 所以要回想你是从那里坠落的，并要悔改，行起初所行的。不然，我就要临到你那里；你若不悔改，我就把你的灯台从原处挪去。 ###EN### Remember therefore where you have fallen from and repent and do the first works; but if not, I am coming to you and will remove your lampstand out of its place, unless you repent.
Verse_Ch: 2:6 然而你有这件事，就是你恨恶尼哥拉党的行为，这也是我所恨恶的。 ###EN### But this you have, that you hate the works of the Nicolaitans, which I also hate.
Verse_Ch: 2:7 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。得胜的，我必将神乐园中生命树的果子赐给他吃。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches. To him who overcomes, to him I will give to eat of the tree of life, which is in the Paradise of God.
Verse_Ch: 2:8 你要写信给在士每拿的召会的使者，说，那首先的、末后的、死过又活的，这样说， ###EN### And to the messenger of the church in Smyrna write: These things says the First and the Last, who became dead and lived again:
Verse_Ch: 2:9 我知道你的患难和贫穷，其实你是富足的，也知道那自称是犹太人，却不是犹太人，乃是撒但会堂的人，所说毁谤的话。 ###EN### I know your tribulation and poverty (but you are rich) and the slander from those who call themselves Jews and are not, but are a synagogue of Satan.
Verse_Ch: 2:10 你将要受的苦你不用怕。看哪，魔鬼将要把你们中间几个人下在监里，叫你们受试炼；你们必受患难十日。你务要至死忠信，我就赐给你那生命的冠 冕。 ###EN### Do not fear the things that you are about to suffer. Behold, the devil is about to cast some of you into prison that you may be tried, and you will have tribulation for ten days. Be faithful unto death, and I will give you the crown of life.
Verse_Ch: 2:11 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。得胜的，绝不会受第二次死的害。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches. He who overcomes shall by no means be hurt of the second death.
Verse_Ch: 2:12 你要写信给在别迦摩的召会的使者，说，那有两刃利剑的，这样说， ###EN### And to the messenger of the church in Pergamos write: These things says He who has the sharp two-edged sword:
Verse_Ch: 2:13 我知道你的居所，就是有撒但座位之处。你持守着我的名，甚至当我忠信的见证人安提帕在你们中间，撒但所住之处被杀的那些日子，你也没有否认 对我的信仰。 ###EN### I know where you dwell, where Satan's throne is; and you hold fast My name and have not denied My faith, even in the days of Antipas, My witness, My faithful one, who was killed among you, where Satan dwells.
Verse_Ch: 2:14 然而有几件事我要责备你，因为在你那里，有人持守巴兰的教训；这巴兰曾教导巴勒，将绊脚石放在以色列子孙面前，叫他们吃祭偶像之物，并且行 淫乱。 ###EN### But I have a few things against you, that you have some there who hold the teaching of Balaam, who taught Balak to put a stumbling block before the sons of Israel, to eat idol sacrifices and to commit fornication.
Verse_Ch: 2:15 你那里也有人照样持守尼哥拉党的教训。 ###EN### In the same way you also have some who hold in like manner the teaching of the Nicolaitans.
Verse_Ch: 2:16 所以你要悔改；不然，我就快临到你那里，用我口中的剑攻击他们。 ###EN### Repent therefore; but if not, I am coming to you quickly, and I will make war with them with the sword of My mouth.
Verse_Ch: 2:17 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。得胜的，我必将那隐藏的吗哪赐给他，并赐他一块白石，上面写着新名，除了那领受的以外，没有人 认识。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches. To him who overcomes, to him I will give of the hidden manna, and to him I will give a white stone, and upon the stone a new name written, which no one knows except him who receives it.
Verse_Ch: 2:18 你要写信给在推雅推喇的召会的使者，说，那眼目如火焰，脚像明亮之铜的神之子，这样说， ###EN### And to the messenger of the church in Thyatira write: These things says the Son of God, He who has eyes like a flame of fire, and His feet are like shining bronze:
Verse_Ch: 2:19 我知道你的行为、爱、信、服事、忍耐，也知道你末后所行的，比起初的更多。 ###EN### I know your works and love and faith and service and your endurance and that your last works are more than the first.
Verse_Ch: 2:20 然而有一件事我要责备你，就是你容让那自称是女申言者的妇人耶洗别教导我的奴仆，引诱他们行淫乱，并吃祭偶像之物。 ###EN### But I have something against you, that you tolerate the woman Jezebel, she who calls herself a prophetess and teaches and leads My slaves astray to commit fornication and to eat idol sacrifices.
Verse_Ch: 2:21 我曾给她时间，让她悔改，她却不肯悔改她的淫行。 ###EN### And I gave her time that she might repent, and she is not willing to repent of her fornication.
Verse_Ch: 2:22 看哪，我要叫她卧病在床，那些与她行淫的人，若不为她所行的悔改，我也要叫他们受大患难。 ###EN### Behold, I cast her into a bed, and those who commit adultery with her, into great tribulation, unless they repent of her works;
Verse_Ch: 2:23 我又要用死亡击杀她的儿女，叫众召会都知道，我是那察看人肺腑心肠的；我且要照你们的行为报应你们各人。 ###EN### And her children I will kill with death; and all the churches will know that I am He who searches the inward parts and the hearts; and I will give to each one of you according to your works.
Verse_Ch: 2:24 至于你们推雅推喇其余的人，就是一切不持有那教训，不明白他们所谓撒但深奥之事的人，我告诉你们，我不将别的重担放在你们身上。 ###EN### But I say to you -- the rest in Thyatira, as many as do not have this teaching, who have not known the deep things of Satan, as they say -- I put no other burden upon you;
Verse_Ch: 2:25 但你们已经有的，总要持守，直等到我来。 ###EN### Nevertheless what you have hold fast until I come.
Verse_Ch: 2:26 得胜的，又守住我的工作到底的，我要赐给他权柄制伏列国； ###EN### And he who overcomes and he who keeps My works until the end, to him I will give authority over the nations;
Verse_Ch: 2:27 他必用铁杖辖管他们，将他们如同窑户的瓦器打得粉碎，像我从我父领受的权柄一样； ###EN### And he will shepherd them with an iron rod, as vessels of pottery are broken in pieces, as I also have received from My Father;
Verse_Ch: 2:28 我又要把晨星赐给他。 ###EN### And to him I will give the morning star.
Verse_Ch: 2:29 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches.
Chapter_Header: 3
Verse_Ch: 3:1 你要写信给在撒狄的召会的使者，说，那有神的七灵和七星的，这样说，我知道你的行为，按名你是活的，其实是死的。 ###EN### And to the messenger of the church in Sardis write: These things says He who has the seven Spirits of God and the seven stars: I know your works, that you have a name that you are living, and yet you are dead.
Verse_Ch: 3:2 你要儆醒，坚固那剩下将要衰微的；因我没有见到你的行为，在我神面前有一样是完成的。 ###EN### Become watchful and establish the things which remain, which were about to die; for I have found none of your works completed before My God.
Verse_Ch: 3:3 所以要回想你是怎样领受，怎样听见的，又要遵守，并要悔改。若不儆醒，我必临到你那里如同贼一样。我几时临到，你也绝不能知道。 ###EN### Remember therefore how you have received and heard, and keep it and repent. If therefore you will not watch, I will come as a thief, and you shall by no means know at what hour I will come upon you.
Verse_Ch: 3:4 然而在撒狄，你还有几名是未曾玷污自己衣服的，他们要穿白衣与我同行，因为他们是配得过的。 ###EN### But you have a few names in Sardis who have not defiled their garments, and they will walk with Me in white because they are worthy.
Verse_Ch: 3:5 得胜的，必这样穿白衣；我也绝不从生命册上涂抹他的名，并且要在我父面前，和我父的众使者面前，承认他的名。 ###EN### He who overcomes will be clothed thus, in white garments, and I shall by no means erase his name out of the book of life, and I will confess his name before My Father and before His angels.
Verse_Ch: 3:6 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches.

Verse_Ch: 3:8 我知道你的行为；看哪，我在你面前给你一个敞开的门，是无人能关的；因为你稍微有一点能力，也曾遵守我的话，没有否认我的名。 ###EN### I know your works; behold, I have put before you an opened door which no one can shut, because you have a little power and have kept My word and have not denied My name.

Verse_Ch: 3:10 你既遵守我忍耐的话，我也必保守你免去那将要临到普天下，试炼一切住在地上之人试炼的时候。 ###EN### Because you have kept the word of My endurance, I also will keep you out of the hour of trial, which is about to come on the whole inhabited earth, to try them who dwell on the earth.
Verse_Ch: 3:11 我必快来，你要持守你所有的，免得有人夺去你的冠冕。 ###EN### I come quickly; hold fast what you have that no one take your crown.
Verse_Ch: 3:12 得胜的，我要叫他在我神殿中作柱子，他也绝不再从那里出去；我又要将我神的名，和我神城的名，（这城就是由天上从我神那里降下来的新耶路撒 冷，）并我的新名，都写在他上面。 ###EN### He who overcomes, him I will make a pillar in the temple of My God, and he shall by no means go out anymore, and I will write upon him the name of My God and the name of the city of My God, the New Jerusalem, which descends out of heaven from My God, and My new name.
Verse_Ch: 3:13 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches.
Verse_Ch: 3:14 你要写信给在老底嘉的召会的使者，说，那阿们，那忠信真实的见证人，那神创造之物的元始，这样说， ###EN### And to the messenger of the church in Laodicea write: These things says the Amen, the faithful and true Witness, the beginning of the creation of God:
Verse_Ch: 3:15 我知道你的行为，你也不冷也不热；我巴不得你或冷或热。 ###EN### I know your works, that you are neither cold nor hot; I wish that you were cold or hot.

Verse_Ch: 3:17 因为你说，我是富足，已经发了财，一样都不缺；却不知道你是那困苦、可怜、贫穷、瞎眼、赤身的。 ###EN### Because you say, I am wealthy and have become rich and have need of nothing, and do not know that you are wretched and miserable and poor and blind and naked,

Verse_Ch: 3:20 看哪，我站在门外叩门；若有听见我声音就开门的，我要进到他那里，我与他，他与我要一同坐席。 ###EN### Behold, I stand at the door and knock; if anyone hears My voice and opens the door, then I will come in to him and dine with him and he with Me.
Verse_Ch: 3:21 得胜的，我要赐他在我宝座上与我同坐，就如我得了胜，在我父的宝座上与祂同坐一样。 ###EN### He who overcomes, to him I will give to sit with Me on My throne, as I also overcame and sat with My Father on His throne.
Verse_Ch: 3:22 那灵向众召会所说的话，凡有耳的，就应当听。 ###EN### He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches.
Chapter_Header: 4

Verse_Ch: 4:3 那位坐着的，显出来的样子好像碧玉和红宝石，又有虹围着宝座，显出来的样子好像绿宝石。 ###EN### And He who was sitting was like a jasper stone and a sardius in appearance, and there was a rainbow around the throne like an emerald in appearance.
Verse_Ch: 4:4 宝座的周围，又有二十四个宝座，其上坐着二十四位长老，身穿白衣，头戴金冠。 ###EN### And around the throne there were twenty-four thrones, and upon the thrones twenty-four elders sitting, clothed in white garments, and upon their heads golden crowns.
Verse_Ch: 4:5 有闪电、声音、雷轰，从宝座中发出。又有七盏火灯在宝座前点着，这七灯就是神的七灵。 ###EN### And out of the throne come forth lightnings and voices and thunders. And there were seven lamps of fire burning before the throne, which are the seven Spirits of God;

Verse_Ch: 21:24 列国要借着城的光行走，地上的君王必将自己的荣耀带进那城。 ###EN### And the nations will walk by its light; and the kings of the earth bring their glory into it.
Verse_Ch: 21:25 城门白昼总不关闭，在那里原没有黑夜。 ###EN### And its gates shall by no means be shut by day, for there will be no night there.
Verse_Ch: 21:26 人必将列国的荣耀尊贵带进那城。 ###EN### And they will bring the glory and the honor of the nations into it.
Verse_Ch: 21:27 凡俗污的，并那行可憎与虚谎之事的，绝不得进那城，只有记在羔羊生命册上的，才得进去。 ###EN### And anything common and he who makes an abomination and a lie shall by no means enter into it, but only those who are written in the Lamb's book of life.
Chapter_Header: 22
Verse_Ch: 22:1 天使又指给我看在城内街道当中一道生命水的河，明亮如水晶，从神和羔羊的宝座流出来。 ###EN### And he showed me a river of water of life, bright as crystal, proceeding out of the throne of God and of the Lamb in the middle of its street.
Verse_Ch: 22:2 在河这边与那边有生命树，生产十二样果子，每月都结出果子，树上的叶子乃为医治万民。 ###EN### And on this side and on that side of the river was the tree of life, producing twelve fruits, yielding its fruit each month; and the leaves of the tree are for the healing of the nations.
Verse_Ch: 22:3 一切咒诅必不再有。在城里有神和羔羊的宝座；祂的奴仆都要事奉祂， ###EN### And there will no longer be a curse. And the throne of God and of the Lamb will be in it, and His slaves will serve Him;
Verse_Ch: 22:4 也要见祂的面；祂的名字必在他们的额上。 ###EN### And they will see His face, and His name will be on their foreheads.
Verse_Ch: 22:5 不再有黑夜，他们也不需要灯光日光，因为主神要光照他们；他们要作王，直到永永远远。 ###EN### And night will be no more; and they have no need of the light of a lamp and of the light of the sun, for the Lord God will shine upon them; and they will reign forever and ever.
Verse_Ch: 22:6 天使又对我说，这些话是可信真实的；主，就是众申言者之灵的神，差遣祂的使者，将那必要快发生的事指示祂的奴仆。 ###EN### And he said to me, These words are faithful and true; and the Lord, the God of the spirits of the prophets, has sent His angel to show to His slaves the things which must quickly take place.
Verse_Ch: 22:7 看哪，我必快来！凡遵守这书上预言之话的有福了。 ###EN### And behold, I come quickly. Blessed is he who keeps the words of the prophecy of this scroll.
Verse_Ch: 22:8 我约翰就是那听见又看见这些事的，我听见又看见了，就在将这些事指给我看的天使脚前，俯伏要拜他。 ###EN### And I John am he who hears and sees these things. And when I heard and saw, I fell to worship before the feet of the angel who showed me these things.
Verse_Ch: 22:9 他对我说，千万不可。我与你、和你的弟兄众申言者、并那些守这书上之话的人，同是作奴仆的，你要敬拜神。 ###EN### And he said to me, Do not do that! I am your fellow slave and a fellow slave of your brothers the prophets and of those who keep the words of this scroll. Worship God.
Verse_Ch: 22:10 他又对我说，不可封住这书上预言的话，因为时候近了。 ###EN### And he said to me, Do not seal the words of the prophecy of this scroll, for the time is near.
Verse_Ch: 22:11 行不义的，叫他仍旧行不义；污秽的，叫他仍旧行污秽；义的，叫他仍旧行义；圣别的，叫他仍旧圣别。 ###EN### Let him who does unrighteousness do unrighteousness still; and let him who is filthy be filthy still; and let him who is righteous do righteousness still; and let him who is holy be holy still.
Verse_Ch: 22:12 看哪，我必快来！赏罚在我，我要照各人所行的报应他。 ###EN### Behold, I come quickly, and My reward is with Me to render to each one as his work is.
Verse_Ch: 22:13 我是阿拉法，我是俄梅嘎；我是首先的，我是末后的；我是初，我是终。 ###EN### I am the Alpha and the Omega, the First and the Last, the Beginning and the End.
Verse_Ch: 22:14 那些洗净自己袍子的有福了，可得权柄到生命树那里，也能从门进城。 ###EN### Blessed are those who wash their robes that they may have right to the tree of life and may enter by the gates into the city.
Verse_Ch: 22:15 城外有那些犬类、行邪术的、淫乱的、杀人的、拜偶像的、和一切喜好并编造谎言的。 ###EN### Outside are the dogs and the sorcerers and the fornicators and the murderers and the idolaters and everyone who loves and makes a lie.
Verse_Ch: 22:16 我耶稣差遣我的使者，为众召会将这些事向你们作见证。我是大卫的根，又是他的后裔，我是明亮的晨星。 ###EN### I Jesus have sent My angel to testify to you these things for the churches. I am the Root and the Offspring of David, the bright morning star.
Verse_Ch: 22:17 那灵和新妇说，来！听见的人也该说，来！口渴的人也当来；愿意的都可以白白取生命的水喝。 ###EN### And the Spirit and the bride say, Come! And let him who hears say, Come! And let him who is thirsty come; let him who wills take the water of life freely.
Verse_Ch: 22:18 我向一切听见这书上预言之话的作见证：若有人在这话上加添什么，神必将写在这书上的灾害加在他身上； ###EN### I testify to everyone who hears the words of the prophecy of this scroll: If anyone adds to them, God will add to him the plagues which are written in this scroll;
Verse_Ch: 22:19 若有人从这书上预言的话删去什么，神必从这书上所写的生命树和圣城，删去他的分。 ###EN### And if anyone takes away from the words of the scroll of this prophecy, God will take away his part from the tree of life and out of the holy city, which are written in this scroll.
Verse_Ch: 22:20 见证这些事的说，是的，我必快来！阿们。主耶稣阿，我愿你来！ ###EN### He who testifies these things says, Yes, I come quickly. Amen. Come, Lord Jesus!
Verse_Ch: 22:21 愿主耶稣的恩与众圣徒同在。阿们。 ###EN### The grace of the Lord Jesus be with all the saints. Amen.
