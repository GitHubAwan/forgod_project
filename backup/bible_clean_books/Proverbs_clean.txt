Verse_Ch: 1:3 使人领受训诲，处事明智，有公义、公平和公正； ###EN### For receiving instruction in wise conduct, Righteousness, justice, and equity;
Verse_Ch: 1:4 使愚蒙人灵明，使少年人有知识和谋略； ###EN### For giving prudence to the simple, Knowledge and discretion to the young man;
Verse_Ch: 1:5 使智慧人听见，增长学识，使聪明人得着智谋； ###EN### That the wise man may hear and increase in learning, And he who has understanding may acquire sound counsel;
Verse_Ch: 1:6 使人明白箴言和譬喻，懂得智慧人的言辞和隐语。 ###EN### For understanding proverb and figure, The words of the wise and their difficult sayings.
Verse_Ch: 1:7 敬畏耶和华是知识的开端；愚妄人藐视智慧和训诲。 ###EN### The fear of Jehovah is the beginning of knowledge; Fools despise wisdom and instruction.
Verse_Ch: 1:8 我儿，要听你父亲的训诲，不可离弃你母亲的指教； ###EN### Hear, my son, the instruction of your father, And do not reject the teaching of your mother;
Verse_Ch: 1:9 因为这要作你头上的华冠，你项上的金链。 ###EN### For they will be a wreath of grace for your head And ornaments for your neck.
Verse_Ch: 1:10 我儿，恶人若引诱你，你不可随从。 ###EN### My son, if sinners entice you, Do not consent.
Verse_Ch: 1:11 他们若说，你与我们同去，我们要埋伏流人的血，要无故蹲伏害无辜的人； ###EN### If they say, Come with us; let us lie in wait for blood; Let us ambush the innocent without cause;
Verse_Ch: 1:12 我们要像阴间把他们活活吞下，把他们如同下坑的人囫囵吞了。 ###EN### Let us swallow them alive like Sheol, And whole, like those who go down into the pit.
Verse_Ch: 1:13 我们必得各样宝物，将所掳来的，装满我们的房屋； ###EN### We will find all manner of precious goods; We will fill our houses with booty;
Verse_Ch: 1:14 你与我们一同有分，大家共用一个囊袋。 ###EN### Throw in your lot with us; We will all have one purse -
Verse_Ch: 1:15 我儿，不要与他们同行这道路；禁止你的脚走他们的途径。 ###EN### My son, do not walk with them on this way; Keep your foot from their path.
Verse_Ch: 1:16 因为他们的脚向恶奔跑；他们急速流人的血。 ###EN### For their feet run toward evil, And they hasten to shed blood.
Verse_Ch: 1:17 好像飞鸟，虽有网罗张设在眼前，仍视而不见； ###EN### For in vain is the net spread In the eyes of any bird;
Verse_Ch: 1:18 这些人埋伏，是为自流己血；蹲伏，是为自害己命。 ###EN### Yet they lie in wait for their own blood; They ambush their own lives!
Verse_Ch: 1:19 凡贪恋财利的，所行之路都是如此；这会夺去得财者的命。 ###EN### Such are the ways of everyone greedy for gain; It takes away the life of its owners.
Verse_Ch: 1:20 智慧在街市上呼喊，在广场上发声。 ###EN### Wisdom cries out in the street; She utters her voice in the open squares.
Verse_Ch: 1:21 在热闹街头喊叫，在城门口，在城里发出言语，说， ###EN### At the head of the tumultuous street she cries; At the entrance of the gates in the city she utters her sayings:
Verse_Ch: 1:22 你们愚蒙人喜爱愚昧，亵慢人喜欢亵慢，愚顽人恨恶知识，要到几时呢？ ###EN### How long, O simple ones, will you love simpleness, And will scoffers delight themselves in scoffing, And will fools hate knowledge?
Verse_Ch: 1:23 你们当因我的责备回转；看哪，我要将我的灵浇灌你们，将我的话指示你们。 ###EN### Turn yourselves at my reproof-Immediately I will pour out my spirit on you; I will make my words known to you.
Verse_Ch: 1:24 我呼唤，你们拒绝；我伸手，无人理会， ###EN### Because I called and you refused; I stretched out my hand and no one heeded,
Verse_Ch: 1:25 反倒轻弃我一切的劝戒，不肯受我的责备。 ###EN### And you neglected all my counsel And would have none of my reproof;
Verse_Ch: 1:26 你们遭灾难，我也必发笑；惊恐临到你们，我必嗤笑。 ###EN### I also will laugh at your calamity; I will mock when what you dread comes,
Verse_Ch: 1:27 惊恐临到你们，好像狂风；灾难来到，如同旋风；急难困苦临到你们身上。 ###EN### When what you dread comes like a storm, And your calamity comes like a whirlwind, When distress and anguish come upon you.
Verse_Ch: 1:28 那时，他们必呼求我，我却不答应；恳切的寻找我，却寻不见。 ###EN### Then they will call on me, but I will not answer; They will seek me diligently, but they will not find me.
Verse_Ch: 1:29 因为他们恨恶知识，不选择敬畏耶和华， ###EN### Because they hated knowledge And did not choose the fear of Jehovah,
Verse_Ch: 1:30 不肯受我的劝戒，藐视我一切的责备， ###EN### And would have none of my counsel And despised all my reproof;
Verse_Ch: 1:31 所以他们必吃自己行径的果子，饱尝自设的计谋。 ###EN### They shall eat of the fruit of their own way And be filled with their own devices.
Verse_Ch: 1:32 愚蒙人背道，必杀己身；愚顽人安逸，必灭己命。 ###EN### For the turning away of the simple will kill them, And the prosperity of the foolish will cause them to perish;
Verse_Ch: 1:33 惟有听从我的，必安然居住，得享安宁，免受灾祸的惊恐。 ###EN### But he who listens to me will dwell securely And will live at ease, without the dread of evil.

Verse_Ch: 2:17 她离弃少年的同伴，忘了神的盟约； ###EN### Who forsakes the companion of her youth And forgets the covenant of her God;

Verse_Ch: 2:20 智慧必使你行善人的道路，守义人的途径。 ###EN### That you may walk in the way of good men And keep the paths of the righteous;
Verse_Ch: 2:21 因为正直人必在地上居住；完全人必在其上存留。 ###EN### For the upright will dwell in the land, And the perfect will remain in it;
Verse_Ch: 2:22 惟有恶人必从地上剪除，奸诈的必从其上拔出。 ###EN### But the wicked will be cut off from the land, And the treacherous will be uprooted from it.
Chapter_Header: 3
Verse_Ch: 3:1 我儿，不要忘记我的指教，你心要谨守我的诫命； ###EN### My son, do not forget my instruction, But let your heart keep my commandments;
Verse_Ch: 3:2 因为它必将长久的日子、生命的年数与平安，加给你。 ###EN### For length of days and years of life And peace will they add to you.
Verse_Ch: 3:3 不可使慈爱、真实离弃你：要系在你颈项上，写在你心版上。 ###EN### Do not let lovingkindness and truth forsake you: Bind them around your neck; Write them upon the tablet of your heart.
Verse_Ch: 3:4 这样，你必在神和人眼前蒙恩宠，有美好的悟性。 ###EN### Thus you will find favor and a reputation for fine understanding In the sight of God and man.
Verse_Ch: 3:5 你要全心信靠耶和华，不可倚靠自己的聪明； ###EN### Trust in Jehovah with all your heart, And do not rely on your own understanding;
Verse_Ch: 3:6 在你一切的道路上，都要认定祂，祂必修直你的途径。 ###EN### In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He will make your paths straight.
Verse_Ch: 3:7 不要自以为有智慧；要敬畏耶和华，远离恶事。 ###EN### Do not be wise in your own eyes; Fear Jehovah, and depart from evil.
Verse_Ch: 3:8 这便医治你的身体，滋润你的骨头。 ###EN### It will be healing to your body And refreshment to your bones.
Verse_Ch: 3:9 你要以财物，和一切初熟的出产，尊荣耶和华。 ###EN### Honor Jehovah with your substance And with the firstfruits of all your produce;
Verse_Ch: 3:10 这样，你的仓房必充满有余，你的酒醡必盈溢新酒。 ###EN### Then your barns will be filled with plenty, And your vats will burst open with new wine.
Verse_Ch: 3:11 我儿，你不可轻看耶和华的惩治，也不可厌烦祂的管教； ###EN### My son, do not despise the chastening of Jehovah, Nor be weary of His discipline;
Verse_Ch: 3:12 因为耶和华所爱的，祂必管教，正如父亲管教所喜爱的儿子。 ###EN### For whom Jehovah loves He disciplines, Even as a father, the son in whom he delights.
Verse_Ch: 3:13 寻得智慧，得着聪明的，这人便为有福。 ###EN### Blessed is the man who finds wisdom, And the man who gains understanding.
Verse_Ch: 3:14 因为赚得智慧胜过赚得银子，其收益强于金子。 ###EN### For her profit is better than the profit of silver, And her income is better than gold.
Verse_Ch: 3:15 智慧比珊瑚更宝贵，你一切所喜爱的，都不足与她比较。 ###EN### She is more precious than corals, And nothing you desire compare with her.
Verse_Ch: 3:16 她右手有长寿，左手有财富与尊荣。 ###EN### Length of days is in her right hand; In her left hand are riches and honor.
Verse_Ch: 3:17 她的道路是安乐的道路；她的路径全是平安。 ###EN### Her ways are ways of pleasantness, And all her paths are peace.
Verse_Ch: 3:18 对持守她的人，她是生命树；持定她的是有福的。 ###EN### She is a tree of life to those who lay hold of her, And happy are those who hold her fast.
Verse_Ch: 3:19 耶和华以智慧立大地，以聪明定诸天； ###EN### Jehovah by wisdom founded the earth; He established the heavens by understanding;
Verse_Ch: 3:20 以知识使深渊裂开，使天空滴下甘露。 ###EN### By His knowledge the depths were broken open And the skies drop down the dew.
Verse_Ch: 3:21 我儿，要谨守真智慧和谋略，不可使这些离开你的眼目。 ###EN### My son, do not let these depart from your sight: Keep sound wisdom and discretion;
Verse_Ch: 3:22 这些必作你魂的生命，作你颈项的美饰。 ###EN### And they will be life for your soul And a graceful ornament for your neck.
Verse_Ch: 3:23 你必安然行路，你的脚必不至绊跌。 ###EN### Then you will walk on your way securely, And your foot will not stumble.
Verse_Ch: 3:24 你躺下，必不惧怕；你躺卧，必睡得香甜。 ###EN### When you lie down, you will not be afraid; But you will lie down, and your sleep will be sweet.
Verse_Ch: 3:25 忽然来的惊恐，你不要害怕；恶人的毁灭临到，你也不要恐惧。 ###EN### Do not be afraid of sudden fear, Nor of the destruction of the wicked when it comes;
Verse_Ch: 3:26 因为耶和华是你所信靠的，祂必保守你的脚不被缠住。 ###EN### For Jehovah will be your confidence, And He will keep your foot from being caught.
Verse_Ch: 3:27 你手若有行善的力量，就不可推辞，当向那应得的人施行。 ###EN### Do not withhold good from those to whom it is due, When it is in the power of your hand to do so.
Verse_Ch: 3:28 你那里若有现成的，不可对邻舍说，去罢，明天再来，我必给你。 ###EN### Do not say to your neighbor, Go, and come back; And tomorrow I will give it-when you have it with you.
Verse_Ch: 3:29 你的邻舍既在你附近安居，你不可设计害他。 ###EN### Do not devise harm against your neighbor, While he lives securely beside you.
Verse_Ch: 3:30 人未曾加害于你，你不可无故与他相争。 ###EN### Do not strive with a man without cause, If he has done you no harm.
Verse_Ch: 3:31 不可嫉妒强暴的人，也不可选择他所行一切的路。 ###EN### Do not envy a man of violence, And do not choose any of his ways;
Verse_Ch: 3:32 因为乖僻人为耶和华所憎恶；祂亲密的指教与正直人同在。 ###EN### For the perverse man is an abomination to Jehovah, But His intimate counsel is with the upright.
Verse_Ch: 3:33 耶和华咒诅恶人的家庭，赐福与义人的居所。 ###EN### The curse of Jehovah is in the house of the wicked man, But He blesses the dwelling place of the righteous.
Verse_Ch: 3:34 祂讥诮那好讥诮的人，赐恩给谦卑的人。 ###EN### To scorners He is scornful, But to the humble He gives grace.
Verse_Ch: 3:35 智慧人必承受尊荣，愚昧人必加添自己的羞辱。 ###EN### The wise will inherit honor, But fools increase their own disgrace.

Verse_Ch: 4:27 不可偏向左右；要使你的脚离开邪恶。 ###EN### Do not turn to the right or to the left; Turn your foot away from evil.
