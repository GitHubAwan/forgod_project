Chapter_Header: 1
Verse_Ch: 1:1 耶和华从会幕中呼叫摩西，对他说， ###EN### Then Jehovah called to Moses and spoke to him out of the Tent of Meeting, saying,
Verse_Ch: 1:2 你要对以色列人说，你们中间若有人献供物给耶和华，要从牛群羊群中献牲畜为供物。 ###EN### Speak to the children of Israel and say to them, When any one of you presents an offering to Jehovah, you shall present your offering from the cattle, of the herd or of the flock.
Verse_Ch: 1:3 他的供物若以牛为燔祭，就要献一只没有残疾的公牛；他要在会幕门口把公牛献上，使他可以在耶和华面前蒙悦纳。 ###EN### If his offering is a burnt offering from the herd, he shall present it, a male without blemish; he shall present it at the entrance of the Tent of Meeting, that he may be accepted before Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 1:4 他要按手在燔祭牲的头上，燔祭牲便蒙悦纳，为他遮罪。 ###EN### And he shall lay his hand on the head of the burnt offering, and it shall be accepted for him, to make expiation for him.
Verse_Ch: 1:5 他要在耶和华面前宰公牛；亚伦子孙作祭司的，要奉上血，把血洒在会幕门口、坛的四边。 ###EN### And he shall slaughter the young bull before Jehovah; and Aaron's sons the priests shall present the blood and sprinkle the blood on and around the altar that is at the entrance of the Tent of Meeting.
Verse_Ch: 1:6 那人要剥去燔祭牲的皮，把燔祭牲切成块子。 ###EN### And he shall skin the burnt offering and cut it into its pieces.
Verse_Ch: 1:7 祭司亚伦的子孙要把火放在坛上，把柴摆列在火上。 ###EN### And the sons of Aaron the priest shall put fire on the altar and arrange the wood in order on the fire.
Verse_Ch: 1:8 亚伦子孙作祭司的，要把肉块、头和脂油，摆列在坛上火的柴上。 ###EN### Then Aaron's sons the priests shall arrange the pieces, the head, and the fat in order on the wood that is on the fire which is on the altar;
Verse_Ch: 1:9 但燔祭牲的内脏与腿，那人要用水洗。祭司要把这一切全烧在坛上，当作燔祭，献与耶和华为怡爽香气的火祭。 ###EN### But its inward parts and its legs he shall wash with water. Then the priest shall burn the whole on the altar, as a burnt offering, an offering by fire, a satisfying fragrance to Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 1:10 人的供物若以羊为燔祭，或绵羊或山羊，就要献上没有残疾的公羊。 ###EN### And if his offering is from the flock, of the sheep or of the goats, for a burnt offering, he shall present it, a male without blemish.
Verse_Ch: 1:11 他要把羊宰于坛的北面，在耶和华面前；亚伦子孙作祭司的，要把血洒在坛的四边。 ###EN### And he shall slaughter it on the side of the altar northward before Jehovah, and Aaron's sons the priests shall sprinkle its blood on and around the altar.
Verse_Ch: 1:12 那人要把燔祭牲切成块子，切下头和脂油，祭司要把这些摆列在坛上火的柴上。 ###EN### And he shall cut it into its pieces with its head and its fat, and the priest shall arrange them in order on the wood that is on the fire which is on the altar;
Verse_Ch: 1:13 但内脏与腿，那人要用水洗。祭司要把这一切全然献上，烧在坛上；这是燔祭，是献与耶和华为怡爽香气的火祭。 ###EN### But the inward parts and the legs he shall wash with water. Then the priest shall present the whole and burn it on the altar; it is a burnt offering, an offering by fire, a satisfying fragrance to Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 1:14 人献给耶和华的供物，若以鸟为燔祭，就要献斑鸠或雏鸽为供物。 ###EN### And if his offering to Jehovah is a burnt offering of birds, then he shall present his offering of turtledoves or of young pigeons.
Verse_Ch: 1:15 祭司要把鸟拿到坛前，揪下头来，把鸟烧在坛上；鸟的血要流在坛的边上； ###EN### And the priest shall present it at the altar and wring off its head and burn it on the altar; and its blood shall be drained out on the side of the altar.
Verse_Ch: 1:16 又要把鸟的嗉囊和羽毛除掉，丢在坛的东面倒灰的地方。 ###EN### And he shall take away its crop with its feathers and cast it beside the altar toward the east, in the place of the ashes.
Verse_Ch: 1:17 要拿着鸟的两个翅膀，把鸟撕开，只是不可撕断；祭司要把鸟放在坛上，在火的柴上焚烧；这是燔祭，是献与耶和华为怡爽香气的火祭 ###EN### Then he shall tear it open by its wings without dividing it, and the priest shall burn it on the altar on the wood that is on the fire; it is a burnt offering, an offering by fire, a satisfying fragrance to Jehovah.
Chapter_Header: 2
Verse_Ch: 2:1 若有人献素祭为供物给耶和华，就要用细面浇上油，加上乳香， ###EN### And when anyone presents an offering of a meal offering to Jehovah, his offering shall be of fine flour; and he shall pour oil on it and put frankincense on it.
Verse_Ch: 2:2 带到亚伦子孙作祭司的那里；祭司就要从细面中取出一把来，并取些油和所有的乳香，然后把所取的这些作为素祭记念的部分，烧在坛上，是献与耶 和华为怡爽香气的火祭。 ###EN### Then he shall bring it to Aaron's sons the priests, and he shall take from it his handful of its fine flour and of its oil with all its frankincense. And the priest shall burn it as its memorial portion on the altar, an offering by fire, a satisfying fragrance to Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 2:3 素祭所剩的要归给亚伦和他的子孙；这是献与耶和华的火祭中为至圣的。 ###EN### And what is left of the meal offering shall be Aaron's and his sons'; it is a thing most holy of Jehovah's offerings by fire.
Verse_Ch: 2:4 你若献炉中烤的物为素祭作供物，就要用细面，作成调油的无酵饼，或抹油的无酵薄饼。 ###EN### And when you present an offering of a meal offering baked in the oven, it shall be of fine flour, unleavened cakes mingled with oil or unleavened wafers anointed with oil.
Verse_Ch: 2:5 你的供物若用煎盘上作的物为素祭，就要用调油的无酵细面， ###EN### And if your offering is a meal offering baked on a flat plate, it shall be of fine flour mingled with oil, unleavened.
Verse_Ch: 2:6 分成块子，浇上油；这是素祭。 ###EN### You shall break it in pieces and pour oil on it; it is a meal offering.
Verse_Ch: 2:7 你的供物若用锅里作的物为素祭，就要用油与细面作成。 ###EN### And if your offering is a meal offering made in a pot, it shall be made of fine flour with oil.
Verse_Ch: 2:8 你要把这些东西作的素祭带到耶和华面前，奉给祭司，带到坛前。 ###EN### And you shall bring to Jehovah the meal offering that is made of these things and present it to the priest, and he shall bring it to the altar.
Verse_Ch: 2:9 祭司要从素祭中取出记念的部分，烧在坛上，是献与耶和华为怡爽香气的火祭。 ###EN### And the priest shall take up from the meal offering its memorial portion and shall burn it on the altar, an offering by fire, a satisfying fragrance to Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 2:10 素祭所剩的要归给亚伦和他的子孙；这是献与耶和华的火祭中为至圣的。 ###EN### And that which is left of the meal offering shall be Aaron's and his sons'; it is a thing most holy of Jehovah's offerings by fire.
Verse_Ch: 2:11 你们献给耶和华的素祭都不可搀酵；因为你们不可烧一点酵、一点蜜当作火祭献给耶和华。 ###EN### No meal offering that you present to Jehovah shall be made with leaven, for you shall not burn any leaven or any honey as an offering by fire to Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 2:12 这些物可以献给耶和华作为初熟的供物，只是不可在坛上献为怡爽的香气。 ###EN### You may present them to Jehovah, as an offering of firstfruits, but they shall not be offered up for a satisfying fragrance on the altar.
Verse_Ch: 2:13 凡献为素祭的供物都要用盐调和，在素祭上不可缺了你神立约的盐；你一切的供物都要配盐而献。 ###EN### And every offering of your meal offering you shall season with salt, and you shall not omit the salt of the covenant of your God from your meal offering; with all your offerings you shall present salt.
Verse_Ch: 2:14 你若向耶和华献初熟之物为素祭，就要献上烘过的青穗粒，就是轧下的新穗粒，当作初熟之物的素祭。 ###EN### And if you present a meal offering of firstfruits to Jehovah, you shall present for the meal offering of your firstfruits new grain roasted with fire, crushed grain of the fresh ear.
Verse_Ch: 2:15 并要加上油，放上乳香；这是素祭。 ###EN### And you shall put oil on it and place frankincense on it; it is a meal offering.
Verse_Ch: 2:16 祭司要把其中作为记念的部分，就是一些轧下的穗粒和一些油，并所有的乳香，都焚烧，当作火祭献给耶和华。 ###EN### And the priest shall burn as its memorial portion, some of its crushed grain and some of its oil, with all its frankincense, as an offering by fire to Jehovah.

Verse_Ch: 27:4 若是女人，你要估定三十舍客勒。 ###EN### And if it is for a female, then your valuation shall be thirty shekels.
Verse_Ch: 27:5 若是从五岁到二十岁，男子你要估定二十舍客勒，女子估定十舍客勒。 ###EN### And if it is for one aged from five years old even to twenty years old, then your valuation for the male shall be twenty shekels and for the female ten shekels.
Verse_Ch: 27:6 若是从一个月到五岁，男子你要估定价银五舍客勒，女子估定价银三舍客勒。 ###EN### And if it is for one aged from a month old even to five years old, then your valuation for the male shall be five shekels of silver, and for the female your valuation shall be three shekels of silver.
Verse_Ch: 27:7 若是从六十岁以上，男人你要估定十五舍客勒，女人估定十舍客勒。 ###EN### And if it is for one aged from sixty years old and upward, if it is for a male, then your valuation shall be fifteen shekels, and for the female ten shekels.
