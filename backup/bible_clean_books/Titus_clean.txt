Chapter_Header: 1
Verse_Ch: 1:1 照着神选民的信仰，与合乎敬虔之真理的知识， ###EN### Paul, a slave of God and an apostle of Jesus Christ according to the faith of God's chosen ones and the full knowledge of the truth, which is according to godliness,
Verse_Ch: 1:2 根据永远生命的盼望，（就是那不能说谎的神，在历世之前所应许， ###EN### In the hope of eternal life, which God, who cannot lie, promised before the times of the ages
Verse_Ch: 1:3 到了适当的时期，借着那按我们救主神的命令所托付我传扬的福音，将祂的话显明出来的，）作神的奴仆、并耶稣基督使徒的保罗， ###EN### But in its own times manifested His word in the proclamation with which I was entrusted according to the command of our Savior God;
Verse_Ch: 1:4 写信给照着共同的信仰，作我真孩子的提多：愿恩典与平安，从父神和我们的救主基督耶稣归与你。 ###EN### To Titus, genuine child according to the common faith: Grace and peace from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Savior.
Verse_Ch: 1:5 我从前留你在革哩底，是要你将我所未办完的事办理妥当，又照我所吩咐你的，在各城设立长老。 ###EN### For this cause I left you in Crete, that you might set in order the things which I have begun that remain and appoint elders in every city, as I directed you:
Verse_Ch: 1:6 若有人无可指责，只作一个妻子的丈夫，儿女是信主的，没有人告他们是放荡、或不服约束的，就可以设立。 ###EN### If anyone is unreprovable, the husband of one wife, having believing children not accused of dissoluteness or unruly.
Verse_Ch: 1:7 因为监督是神的管家，必须无可指责，不任性，不暴躁，不醉酒滋事，不打人，不贪卑鄙的利益； ###EN### For the overseer must be unreprovable as a steward of God, not self-willed, not quick tempered, not an excessive drinker, not a striker, not greedy for base gain;
Verse_Ch: 1:8 却乐意待客，喜爱良善，清明自守，义、圣、节制； ###EN### But hospitable, a lover of good, of a sober mind, righteous, holy, self-controlled;
Verse_Ch: 1:9 坚守那按照使徒教训可信靠的话，好能用健康的教训劝勉人，又能使那些反对的人知罪自责。 ###EN### Holding to the faithful word, which is according to the teaching of the apostles, that he may be able both to exhort by the healthy teaching and to convict those who oppose.
Verse_Ch: 1:10 因为有许多人不服约束，说虚空的话，欺哄人，那些奉割礼的人更是这样， ###EN### For there are many unruly men, vain talkers and deceivers, especially those of the circumcision,
Verse_Ch: 1:11 这些人的口必须堵住；他们为了卑鄙的利益，将不当教导的事教导人，就倾覆了人的全家。 ###EN### Whose mouths must be stopped, who overthrow whole households, teaching things for the sake of base gain, which they ought not to do.
Verse_Ch: 1:12 有革哩底人中间的一位，就是他们自己的申言者，曾说，革哩底人常是说谎的，乃是恶兽，又馋又懒。 ###EN### One from among themselves, a prophet of their own, said, Cretans are always liars, evil beasts, idle gluttons.
Verse_Ch: 1:13 这个见证是真的；为这缘故，你要严厉的责备他们，使他们在信仰上健康， ###EN### This testimony is true; for which cause reprove them severely that they may be healthy in the faith,
Verse_Ch: 1:14 不注意犹太人虚构无稽之事，和转离真理之人的诫命。 ###EN### Not paying attention to Jewish myths and the commandments of men who turn away from the truth.
Verse_Ch: 1:15 对洁净的人，凡物都洁净；但对污秽不信的人，什么都不洁净，反而连他们的心思和良心，也都污秽了。 ###EN### All things are pure to the pure; yet to those who are defiled and unbelieving nothing is pure, but both their mind and their conscience are defiled.
Verse_Ch: 1:16 他们自称认识神，却在行为上否认祂，本是可憎恶的、悖逆的，在各样善事上是不蒙称许的。 ###EN### They profess to know God, but by their works they deny Him, being abominable and disobedient and, as to every good work, disapproved.
Chapter_Header: 2
Verse_Ch: 2:1 至于你，要讲那合乎健康教训的话。 ###EN### But you, speak the things which are fitting to the healthy teaching.
Verse_Ch: 2:2 劝老年人要节制适度、庄重、清明自守，在信、爱、忍耐上都要健康。 ###EN### Exhort older men to be temperate, grave, of a sober mind, healthy in faith, in love, in endurance;
Verse_Ch: 2:3 劝老年妇人也是一样，在举止行动上，要和有分于圣事的人相称，不说谗言，不被酒奴役，将善美的事教导人， ###EN### Older women likewise to be in demeanor as befits those who engage in sacred things, not slanderers, nor enslaved by much wine, teachers of what is good,
Verse_Ch: 2:4 好训练年轻的妇人爱丈夫，爱儿女， ###EN### That they may train the young women to love their husbands, to love their children,
Verse_Ch: 2:5 清明自守，贞洁，料理家务，良善，服从自己的丈夫，免得神的话被毁谤。 ###EN### To be of a sober mind, pure, workers at home, good, subject to their own husbands, that the word of God would not be blasphemed.
Verse_Ch: 2:6 劝青年人也是一样，要清明自守。 ###EN### The younger men likewise exhort to be of a sober mind,
Verse_Ch: 2:7 在凡事上你自己要显出善行的榜样，在教导上要不腐化，要庄重， ###EN### Concerning all things presenting yourself as a pattern of good works: in your teaching showing incorruption, gravity,
Verse_Ch: 2:8 要用无可挑剔的健康言语，使那反对的人既没有坏事可说到我们，便自觉羞愧。 ###EN### Healthy speech that cannot be condemned, that he who opposes may be put to shame, having nothing evil to say about us.
Verse_Ch: 2:9 劝奴仆要凡事服从自己的主人，讨他们的喜欢，不可顶撞他们， ###EN### Exhort slaves to be subject to their own masters in all things, to be well pleasing, not contradicting,
Verse_Ch: 2:10 不可私拿东西，却要显为绝对优良可信，好使我们救主神的教训在凡事上都得光荣。 ###EN### Not pilfering, but showing all good faithfulness that they may adorn the teaching of our Savior God in all things.
Verse_Ch: 2:11 因为神救万人的恩典，已经显现出来， ###EN### For the grace of God, bringing salvation to all men, has appeared,
Verse_Ch: 2:12 教导我们弃绝不敬虔和属世的情欲，好在今世过自守、公义、敬虔的生活， ###EN### Training us that, denying ungodliness and worldly lusts, we should live soberly and righteously and godly in the present age,
Verse_Ch: 2:13 等候那有福的盼望，就是至大的神和我们的救主，耶稣基督之荣耀的显现。 ###EN### Awaiting the blessed hope, even the appearing of the glory of our great God and Savior, Jesus Christ,
Verse_Ch: 2:14 祂为我们舍了自己，要赎我们脱离一切的不法，并洁净我们，归祂自己成为独特的子民，作祂特有的产业，热心行善。 ###EN### Who gave Himself for us that He might redeem us from all lawlessness and purify to Himself a particular people as His unique possession, zealous of good works.
Verse_Ch: 2:15 这些事你要讲说，也要用各等权柄劝勉人、责备人；不可叫人轻看你。 ###EN### These things speak, and exhort and convict with all authority. Let no one despise you.
Chapter_Header: 3
Verse_Ch: 3:1 你要提醒众人服从执政的、掌权的，顺从他们，预备行各样的善事。 ###EN### Remind them to be subject to rulers, to authorities, to be obedient, to be ready unto every good work,
Verse_Ch: 3:2 不要毁谤人，不要争竞，乃要谦让宜人，向众人显出十分的温柔。 ###EN### To slander no one, to be uncontentious, gentle, showing all meekness toward all men.
Verse_Ch: 3:3 因为我们从前也是无知、悖逆、受迷惑的，给各样的私欲和宴乐作奴仆，将生命时光耗费于恶毒和嫉妒，是可恨的，又是彼此相恨。 ###EN### For we also were once foolish, disobedient, deceived, serving as slaves various lusts and pleasures, spending our lives in malice and envy, hateful, hating one another.
Verse_Ch: 3:4 然而，当我们救主神的恩慈，和祂对人的爱显现的时候， ###EN### But when the kindness and the love to man of our Savior God appeared,
Verse_Ch: 3:5 祂便救了我们，并不是本于我们所成就的义行，乃是照着祂的怜悯，借着重生的洗涤，和圣灵的更新。 ###EN### Not out of works in righteousness which we did but according to His mercy He saved us, through the washing of regeneration and the renewing of the Holy Spirit,
Verse_Ch: 3:6 这圣灵就是神借着耶稣基督我们的救主，丰丰富富浇灌在我们身上的， ###EN### Whom He poured out upon us richly through Jesus Christ our Savior,
Verse_Ch: 3:7 好叫我们既因祂的恩典得称义，就可以照着永远生命的盼望成为后嗣。 ###EN### In order that having been justified by His grace, we might become heirs according to the hope of eternal life.
Verse_Ch: 3:8 这话是可信的，我也愿你把这些事确实坚信的讲明，使那些已信神的人留心作正经事业。这些都是美事，并且与人有益。 ###EN### Faithful is the word, and concerning these things I desire you to confidently affirm, so that those who have believed in God may be careful to maintain respectable works. These things are good and profitable to men.
Verse_Ch: 3:9 但要远避愚拙的辩论、家谱、争竞、以及因律法而起的争论，因为这些都是无益虚妄的。 ###EN### But avoid foolish questionings and genealogies and strifes and contentions about the law, for they are unprofitable and vain.
Verse_Ch: 3:10 分门结党的人，警戒过一两次，就要拒绝； ###EN### A factious man, after a first and second admonition, refuse,
Verse_Ch: 3:11 知道这等人已经深入歧途，虽定自己的罪，仍是犯罪。 ###EN### Knowing that such a one is perverted and is sinning, though he is condemned by his own self.
Verse_Ch: 3:12 我打发亚提马或推基古到你那里去的时候，你要赶紧往尼哥波立去见我，因为我已经定意在那里过冬。 ###EN### When I send Artemas to you or Tychicus, be diligent to come to me at Nicopolis, for I have decided to spend the winter there.
Verse_Ch: 3:13 你要赶紧给律法师西纳和亚波罗送行，叫他们没有缺乏。 ###EN### Zenas the lawyer and Apollos send forward diligently that nothing may be lacking to them.
Verse_Ch: 3:14 并且我们的人也要学习作正经事业，好应必需，免得不结果子。 ###EN### And let those also who are ours learn to maintain respectable works for their necessary needs, that they may not be unfruitful.
Verse_Ch: 3:15 同我在一起的众人都问你安。请代问那些因信爱我们的人安。愿恩典与你们众人同在。 ###EN### All who are with me greet you. Greet those who love us in faith. Grace be with you all.
