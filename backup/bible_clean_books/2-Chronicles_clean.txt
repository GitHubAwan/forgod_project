Chapter_Header: 1
Verse_Ch: 1:1 大卫的儿子所罗门国位坚固；耶和华他的神与他同在，使他甚为尊大。 ###EN### And Solomon the son of David was strengthened in his kingdom, and Jehovah his God was with him and magnified him exceedingly.
Verse_Ch: 1:2 所罗门吩咐以色列众人，就是千夫长、百夫长、审判官、以及全以色列所有作宗族首领的领袖都来。 ###EN### And Solomon spoke to all Israel, to the captains of thousands and of hundreds and to the judges and to every leader of all Israel, the heads of the fathers' houses.
Verse_Ch: 1:3 所罗门带同全会众往基遍的邱坛去，因那里有神的会幕，就是耶和华仆人摩西在旷野所制造的。 ###EN### And Solomon and all the assembly with him went up to the high place that was in Gibeon, for the Tent of Meeting of God was there, which Moses the servant of Jehovah had made in the wilderness.
Verse_Ch: 1:4 只是神的约柜，大卫已经从基列耶琳运上去，到他为约柜所预备的地方，因他曾在耶路撒冷为约柜支搭了帐幕。 ###EN### However David had brought up the Ark of God from Kiriath-jearim to the place that David had prepared for it, for he had pitched a tent for it in Jerusalem.
Verse_Ch: 1:5 户珥的孙子，乌利的儿子比撒列所造的铜坛，也在基遍耶和华的会幕前；所罗门和会众都在坛前求问。 ###EN### And the bronze altar that Bezalel the son of Uri, the son of Hur, had made was there before the tabernacle of Jehovah, and Solomon and the assembly inquired at it.
Verse_Ch: 1:6 所罗门上到耶和华面前会幕的铜坛那里，在坛上献一千燔祭牲。 ###EN### And Solomon went up there before Jehovah to the bronze altar, which was at the Tent of Meeting, and he offered upon it one thousand burnt offerings.
Verse_Ch: 1:7 当夜，神向所罗门显现，对他说，我当赐给你什么？你可以求。 ###EN### During that night God appeared to Solomon and said to him, Ask what I should give you.
Verse_Ch: 1:8 所罗门对神说，你曾向我父亲大卫大施恩慈，使我接续他作王。 ###EN### And Solomon said to God, You have shown great lovingkindness to David my father and have made me king in his place.
Verse_Ch: 1:9 耶和华神阿，现在求你使你向我父亲大卫所说的话得以实现；因你立我作这民的王，他们如同地上的尘沙那样多。 ###EN### And now, O Jehovah God, may Your promise to David my father be fulfilled, for You have made me king over a people as numerous as the dust of the earth.
Verse_Ch: 1:10 求你赐我智慧和知识，我好在这民前出入；不然，谁能审断你这众多的民呢？ ###EN### Now give me wisdom and knowledge, that I may go out and come in before this people; for who can judge this great people of Yours?
Verse_Ch: 1:11 神对所罗门说，你心里既有此意，并不求财富、资产、尊荣，也不求灭绝那恨你之人的性命，又不求长寿，只为自己求智慧和知识，好审断我的民， 就是我立你作王来治理的； ###EN### Then God said to Solomon, Because this is on your heart and you have not asked for riches, wealth, or honor, nor for the life of those who hate you, nor have you even asked for long life, but have asked for wisdom and knowledge for yourself so that you may judge My people over whom I have made you king;
Verse_Ch: 1:12 我必赐你智慧和知识，也必赐你财富、资产、尊荣，在你以前的列王都没有这样，在你以后的也必没有这样。 ###EN### Wisdom and knowledge are granted to you; and riches and wealth and honor I will give you, such as no kings who were before you have had, nor any after you will have.
Verse_Ch: 1:13 于是，所罗门从基遍的邱坛，从会幕前回到耶路撒冷，治理以色列。 ###EN### Then Solomon came to Jerusalemfrom the high place that was in Gibeon, from the Tent of Meeting; and he reigned over Israel.
Verse_Ch: 1:14 所罗门聚集战车马兵，有战车一千四百辆，马兵一万二千名，安置在屯车城，并在耶路撒冷，与王在一起。 ###EN### And Solomon gathered chariots and horsemen together; and he had one thousand four hundred chariots and twelve thousand horsemen, which he stationed in the chariot cities and with the king at Jerusalem.
Verse_Ch: 1:15 王在耶路撒冷使金银多如石头，香柏木多如低陆的桑树。 ###EN### And the king caused silver and gold to be as plentiful as stones in Jerusalem; and cedars, like the sycamores that are in the lowlands.
Verse_Ch: 1:16 所罗门的马出自埃及和古厄，是王的商人从古厄按着定价买来的。 ###EN### And the horses that Solomon had came from Egypt and from Kue; the king's traders bought them from Kue at a fixed price.
Verse_Ch: 1:17 他们从埃及买来的车，每辆价银六百舍客勒，马每匹一百五十舍客勒；赫人诸王和亚兰诸王所买的车马，也是这样经他们手带出来的。 ###EN### And they could import from Egypt a chariot for six hundred shekels of silver and a horse for one hundred fifty shekels, and thus they brought them out to all the kings of the Hittites and the kings of Syria by their own means.
Chapter_Header: 2
Verse_Ch: 2:1 所罗门定意为耶和华的名建造殿宇，又为自己的国建造宫室。 ###EN### Now Solomon purposed to build a house for the name of Jehovah and his royal palace.
Verse_Ch: 2:2 所罗门数点出七万人扛抬重物，八万人在山上凿石头，三千六百人督管他们。 ###EN### And Solomon counted out seventy thousand men to bear burdens and eighty thousand men as stonecutters in the mountains and three thousand six hundred men to oversee them.
Verse_Ch: 2:3 所罗门差人去见推罗王户兰，说，你怎样待我父亲大卫，运香柏木给他建宫居住，求你也这样待我。 ###EN### And Solomon sent word to Huram the king of Tyre, saying, As you did to David my father, in that you sent him cedars to build a house for him to dwell in, so do for me.
Verse_Ch: 2:4 看哪，我要为耶和华我神的名建造殿宇，分别为圣献给祂，在祂面前烧馨香的香，常摆陈设饼，每早晚、安息日、月朔、并耶和华我们神所定的节期 献燔祭；这是以色列永远的定例。 ###EN### I am now about to build a house for the name of Jehovah my God to sanctify to Him, for the burning of fragrant incense before Him and the arranging of the rows of bread continually and the offering up of burnt offerings in the morning and in the evening, on Sabbaths and on new moons and on the appointed feasts of Jehovah our God, this being an ordinance for Israel forever.
Verse_Ch: 2:5 我所要建造的殿宇甚大；因为我们的神大于诸神。 ###EN### And the house which I am building will be great; for greater is our God than all the gods.
Verse_Ch: 2:6 但谁能为祂建造殿宇呢？天和天上的天尚且容不下祂。我是谁？能为祂建造殿宇么？不过在祂面前烧香而已。 ###EN### But who is able to build Him a house? For the heavens and the heaven of heavens is not able to contain Him. And who am I, that I should build Him a house, except to burn incense before Him?
Verse_Ch: 2:7 现在求你差一个人来，就是善用金、银、铜、铁，和紫色、朱红色、蓝色线，并精于雕刻之工的巧匠，与我父亲大卫所预备，在犹大和耶路撒冷我这 里的巧匠一同作工。 ###EN### So now send me a man, skillful in working with gold and with silver and with bronze and with iron and with purple and crimson and blue cloth, and who knows how to make engravings, to be with the skillful men who are with me in Judah and in Jerusalem, whom David my father prepared.
Verse_Ch: 2:8 又求你从利巴嫩运些香柏木、松木、檀香木到我这里来，因我知道你的仆人善于砍伐利巴嫩的树木。我的仆人必与你的仆人一同作工， ###EN### Send me timber of cedar, cypress, and algum from Lebanon, for I know that your servants know how to cut the timber of Lebanon. And now my servants will be with your servants,
Verse_Ch: 2:9 好给我预备许多木料，因我要建造的殿宇伟大美妙。 ###EN### To prepare for me timber in abundance; for the house which I am building will be great and wonderful.
Verse_Ch: 2:10 至于你的仆人，就是砍伐树木的工人，我必给他们打好了的小麦二万歌珥，大麦二万歌珥，酒二万罢特，油二万罢特。 ###EN### And now I will give to your servants, the hewers who cut timber, twenty thousand cors of crushed wheat and twenty thousand cors of barley and twenty thousand baths of wine and twenty thousand baths of oil.
Verse_Ch: 2:11 推罗王户兰写信送给所罗门，说，耶和华因为爱祂的子民，所以立你作他们的王； ###EN### And Huram the king of Tyre answered by letter, sending it to Solomon, saying, Because Jehovah loves His people, He has made you king over them.
Verse_Ch: 2:12 户兰又说，造作诸天与地的耶和华以色列的神是当受颂赞的；祂赐给大卫王一个有智慧的儿子，满有见识和悟性，可以为耶和华建造殿宇，又为自己 的国建造宫室。 ###EN### Huram also said, Blessed be Jehovah the God of Israel, who made the heavens and the earth, who has given King David a wise son endowed with discernment and understanding, who will build a house for Jehovah and his royal palace.
Verse_Ch: 2:13 现在我打发一个精巧、满有悟性的人去，就是户兰亚比， ###EN### And now I have sent a skillful man, endowed with understanding, Huram-abi,
Verse_Ch: 2:14 他是但支派一个妇人的儿子，父亲是推罗人；他善用金、银、铜、铁、石、木，和紫色、蓝色线、细麻与朱红色线制造各物，并精于各种雕刻，又能 设计各样交托给他的巧工；他可以与你的巧匠和你父亲我主大卫的巧匠一同作工。 ###EN### The son of a woman of the daughters of Dan, whose father is a man of Tyre, who knows how to work in gold and in silver and in bronze and in iron, in stones and in wood, in purple, in blue cloth, and in fine linen and in crimson cloth and who knows how to make all kinds of engraving, and to fashion any design which is given to him, to work with your skillful men and the skillful men of my lord David your father.
Verse_Ch: 2:15 我主所说的小麦、大麦、油、酒，愿我主运来给众仆人； ###EN### And now let my lord send to his servants the wheat and the barley, the oil and the wine of which he spoke;
Verse_Ch: 2:16 我们必照你一切所需的，从利巴嫩砍伐树木，扎成筏子，浮海运到约帕。你可以从那里运上耶路撒冷去。 ###EN### And we will cut whatever timber you need from Lebanon and bring it to you on rafts by sea to Joppa, and you can bring it up to Jerusalem.
Verse_Ch: 2:17 所罗门仿照他父亲大卫所作的，数点所有在以色列地寄居的人，共得十五万三千六百名； ###EN### And Solomon numbered all the men who were sojourners who were in the land of Israel after the census which David his father had made of them, and they found one hundred fifty-three thousand six hundred.
Verse_Ch: 2:18 他使其中七万人扛抬重物，八万人在山上凿石头，三千六百人督理百姓作工。 ###EN### And he appointed seventy thousand of them as burden bearers and eighty thousand as stonecutters in the mountains, and three thousand six hundred overseers to make the people work.
Chapter_Header: 3
Verse_Ch: 3:1 所罗门在耶路撒冷，耶和华向他父亲大卫显现的摩利亚山上，就是耶布斯人阿珥楠的禾场上，大卫所预备的地方，开工建造耶和华的殿。 ###EN### And Solomon began to build the house of Jehovah in Jerusalem on Mount Moriah, where He had appeared to David his father, at the place that David prepared, on the threshing floor of Ornan the Jebusite.
Verse_Ch: 3:2 所罗门作王第四年二月初二日开工建造。 ###EN### And he began to build on the second day of the second month in the fourth year of his reign.
Verse_Ch: 3:3 所罗门建造神殿所立的根基，乃是这样：长六十肘，宽二十肘，都按着古时的尺寸。 ###EN### And these are the foundations which Solomon laid to build the house of God. The length in cubits, according to the former standard, was sixty cubits, and it was twenty cubits wide.
Verse_Ch: 3:4 殿前的廊子长二十肘，与殿的宽度一样，高一百二十肘；里面贴上纯金。 ###EN### And the portico that was at the front was as long as the width of the house, twenty cubits; and its height was one hundred twenty cubits. And he overlaid it within with pure gold.
Verse_Ch: 3:5 大殿的墙都铺上松木，又贴上精金，上面雕刻棕树和链子。 ###EN### And the greater house he overlaid with cypress wood, and he overlaid it with fine gold and put palm trees and chains on it.
Verse_Ch: 3:6 又用宝石装饰殿墙，使殿华美；所用的金子都是巴瓦音的金子。 ###EN### And he adorned the house with precious stones for beauty, and the gold was gold from Parvaim.
Verse_Ch: 3:7 又用金子贴殿的栋梁、门槛、墙壁、门扇；墙上雕刻基路伯。 ###EN### And he overlaid the house-the beams, the thresholds, and its walls and its doors-with gold; and he carved cherubim on the walls.
Verse_Ch: 3:8 又建造至圣所，长二十肘，与殿的宽度一样，宽也是二十肘；贴上精金，共用金子六百他连得。 ###EN### And he made the house of the Holy of Holies; its length, across the width of the house, was twenty cubits, and its width was twenty cubits; and he overlaid it with fine gold amounting to six hundred talents.
Verse_Ch: 3:9 钉子是金的，重五十舍客勒；楼房都贴上金子。 ###EN### And the weight of the nails was fifty shekels of gold, and the upper chambers he overlaid with gold.
Verse_Ch: 3:10 在至圣所按造像的法子作两个基路伯，用金子包裹。 ###EN### And he made two cherubim of sculptured work in the house of the Holy of Holies, and they overlaid them with gold.
Verse_Ch: 3:11 两个基路伯的翅膀共长二十肘。这一个基路伯的一个翅膀长五肘，触着殿这边的墙；那一个翅膀也长五肘，与那基路伯翅膀相接。 ###EN### And the wingspan of the cherubim was twenty cubits; the wing of one, of five cubits, touched the wall of the house, and its other wing, of five cubits, touched the wing of the other cherub.
Verse_Ch: 3:12 那基路伯的一个翅膀长五肘，触着殿那边的墙；那一个翅膀也长五肘，紧接着第一个基路伯的翅膀。 ###EN### And the wing of the other cherub, of five cubits, touched the wall of the house, and its other wing, of five cubits, was attached to the wing of the first cherub.
Verse_Ch: 3:13 这两个基路伯张开翅膀，共长二十肘，面向外殿而立。 ###EN### The wings of these cherubim were spread out twenty cubits in length; and they stood on their feet, and their faces were toward the house.
Verse_Ch: 3:14 又用蓝色、紫色、朱红色线，和细麻织幔子，在其上绣着基路伯。 ###EN### And he made the veil of blue and purple and crimson cloth and fine linen, and he put cherubim upon it.
Verse_Ch: 3:15 在殿前造了两根柱子，高三十五肘；每柱子上端的柱顶高五肘。 ###EN### And at the front of the house he made two pillars, thirty-five cubits high; and the capital that was on the top of each was five cubits high.
Verse_Ch: 3:16 又在内殿作链子，安在柱子上端；又作一百个石榴，安在链子上。 ###EN### And he made chains in the innermost sanctuary and set them on the tops of the pillars, and he made one hundred pomegranates and put them on the chains.
Verse_Ch: 3:17 将两根柱子立在殿前，一根在右边，一根在左边；右边的起名叫雅斤，左边的起名叫波阿斯。 ###EN### And he erected the pillars in front of the temple, one on the right and one on the left; and he called the name of the one on the right Jachin and the name of the one on the left Boaz.
Chapter_Header: 4
Verse_Ch: 4:1 他又制造一座铜坛，长二十肘，宽二十肘，高十肘。 ###EN### And he made an altar of bronze; its length was twenty cubits, and its width was twenty cubits, and its height was ten cubits.
Verse_Ch: 4:2 又铸一个铜海，直径十肘，样式是圆的，高五肘，围三十肘。 ###EN### And he made the molten sea, ten cubits from brim to brim, fully round; and it was five cubits high, and a line of thirty cubits encompassed it.

Verse_Ch: 4:5 海厚一掌，其边如杯边的作法，如百合花，可容三千罢特。 ###EN### And it was a handbreadth thick; and its brim was like the work of a cup's brim, like the flower of a lily; it could hold three thousand baths.
Verse_Ch: 4:6 又制造十个盆，可在其中洗濯，五个放在右边，五个放在左边，献燔祭所用之物要在盆中清洗；但海是为祭司洗濯用的。 ###EN### And he made ten basins in which to wash, to rinse the things for the burnt offering, and he set five on the right and five on the left; but the sea was for the priests to wash in.
Verse_Ch: 4:7 他又照所定的样式造十个金灯台放在殿里，五个在右边，五个在左边。 ###EN### Then he made the ten golden lampstands according to the ordinance for them, and he put them in the temple, five on the right and five on the left.
Verse_Ch: 4:8 又造十张桌子放在殿里，五张在右边，五张在左边；又造一百个金碗。 ###EN### And he made ten tables and placed them in the temple, five on the right and five on the left; and he made one hundred golden bowls.
Verse_Ch: 4:9 又造祭司院、大院和大院的门，用铜包裹门扇。 ###EN### And he made the court of the priests and the great court and the doors of the court, and he overlaid their doors with bronze.
Verse_Ch: 4:10 将海安在殿的右边，就是在东南边。 ###EN### And he put the sea on the right side of the house, on the east side southward.
Verse_Ch: 4:11 户兰又造了锅、铲和碗。这样，户兰完成了他为所罗门王所作神殿中的工。 ###EN### And Huram made the pots and the shovels and the bowls. Thus Huram finished doing the work that he was doing for King Solomon in the house of God:
Verse_Ch: 4:12 所造的就是：两根柱子，和柱子上端柱顶的两个球，并两个盖着柱子上端柱顶两个球的网子； ###EN### The two pillars and the bowls and the two capitals that were at the top of the pillars and the two networks to cover the two bowls of the capitals that were at the top of the pillars;
Verse_Ch: 4:13 四百个石榴，安在两个网子上，每网两行石榴，盖着柱子上端柱顶的两个球。 ###EN### And the four hundred pomegranates for the two networks, two rows of pomegranates for each network, to cover the two bowls of the capitals that were on the pillars.
Verse_Ch: 4:14 又造盆座，并造其上的盆； ###EN### He also made the bases, and he made the basins upon the bases;
Verse_Ch: 4:15 一个海和海下的十二只牛； ###EN### And the one sea and the twelve oxen under it.
Verse_Ch: 4:16 锅、铲、肉叉、与一切相关的器具，都是户兰亚比用磨亮的铜给所罗门王为耶和华的殿所造的， ###EN### And the pots and the shovels and the forks and all their vessels that Huram-abi made for King Solomon for the house of Jehovah were of burnished bronze.
Verse_Ch: 4:17 是王在约但平原，在疏割和撒利但之间用胶泥铸成的。 ###EN### The king cast them in the plain of the Jordan, in the clay ground between Succoth and Zeredah.
Verse_Ch: 4:18 所罗门所制造的这一切器具，数量甚多，铜的重量无法可查。 ###EN### And Solomon made all these vessels in great number, for the weight of bronze could not be ascertained.
Verse_Ch: 4:19 所罗门又造神殿里的一切器具：金坛和摆陈设饼的桌子； ###EN### And Solomon made all the vessels that were in the house of God: the golden altar; and the tables upon which the bread of the Presence was put;
Verse_Ch: 4:20 纯金的灯台和灯盏，可以照例点在内殿前； ###EN### And the lampstands and their lamps of pure gold to burn according to their ordinance before the innermost sanctuary;
Verse_Ch: 4:21 还有灯台上的花、灯盏和灯剪，都是金的，且是最纯的金； ###EN### And the flowers and the lamps and the tongs, of gold, of purest gold;
Verse_Ch: 4:22 又有镊子、碗、调羹、火盆，都是纯金的；以及殿的入口、最里面属至圣所的门扇、和外殿的门扇，都是金的。 ###EN### And the snuffers and the bowls and the spoons and the firepans, of pure gold; and the entrance of the house, its innermost doors for the Holy of Holies, and the doors of the house of the temple, of gold.
Chapter_Header: 5
Verse_Ch: 5:1 这样，所罗门为耶和华殿所作的一切工完成了。所罗门把他父亲大卫分别为圣之物，就是银子、金子和器具，都带来放在神殿的府库里。 ###EN### Thus all the work that Solomon did for the house of Jehovah was finished. Verse_Ch: 30:19 凡心中预备好寻求神，就是耶和华他列祖之神的，虽不照着圣所洁净之礼自洁，求至善的耶和华也宽恕他。 ###EN### Who has prepared his heart to seek after God, after Jehovah, the God of his fathers, even though he is not according to the rules of purification for the sanctuary.
Verse_Ch: 30:20 耶和华垂听希西家的祷告，就饶恕百姓。 ###EN### And Jehovah heard Hezekiah and healed the people.
Verse_Ch: 30:21 在耶路撒冷的以色列人大大喜乐，守除酵节七日；利未人和祭司用响亮的乐器，日日赞美耶和华。 ###EN### And the children of Israel who were found at Jerusalem held the Feast of Unleavened Bread for seven days with great rejoicing, and the Levites and the priests praised Jehovah day by day, with loud instruments played to Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 30:22 希西家慰勉一切善于事奉耶和华的利未人；于是众人吃节期的筵席七日，献平安祭，又称颂耶和华他们列祖的神。 ###EN### And Hezekiah spoke encouragingly to all the Levites who showed themselves well-skilled in their service to Jehovah, and so they ate the food of the appointed feast for seven days, sacrificing sacrifices of peace offerings and extolling Jehovah, the God of their fathers.
Verse_Ch: 30:23 全会众商议，要再守节七日；于是欢欢喜喜的又守节七日。 ###EN### And the whole congregation took counsel to hold the feast another seven days, and they held the feast those seven days with rejoicing.
Verse_Ch: 30:24 犹大王希西家赐给会众公牛一千只，羊七千只为祭物；众首领也赐给会众公牛一千只，羊一万只，并有许多的祭司使自己分别为圣。 ###EN### For Hezekiah the king of Judah contributed one thousand bulls and seven thousand sheep to the congregation, and the leaders contributed one thousand bulls and ten thousand sheep to the congregation, and a great number of priests sanctified themselves.
Verse_Ch: 30:25 犹大全会众、祭司、利未人、并那从以色列来的会众、以及从以色列地来住在犹大的寄居者，尽都喜乐。 ###EN### And all the congregation of Judah with the priests and the Levites and all the congregation who came from Israel and the sojourners who came from the land of Israel and dwelt in Judah rejoiced,
Verse_Ch: 30:26 这样，在耶路撒冷大有喜乐，自从以色列王大卫儿子所罗门的时候，在耶路撒冷没有这样的喜乐。 ###EN### And there was great rejoicing in Jerusalem, for since the days of Solomon the son of David, the king of Israel, there had not been the like in Jerusalem.
Verse_Ch: 30:27 那时，祭司利未人起来，为民祝福；他们的声音蒙神垂听，他们的祷告达到天上祂的圣所。 ###EN### Then the Levitical priests rose up and blessed the people; and their voice was heard and their prayer went up to His sanctuary, to heaven.
Chapter_Header: 31
Verse_Ch: 31:1 这事既都完毕，在那里的以色列众人就出到犹大的诸城邑，打碎柱像，砍下木像，又在全犹大、便雅悯、以法莲和玛拿西，将邱坛和祭坛拆毁净尽。 于是以色列众人都回到自己的城，各归自己的地业。 ###EN### And when all this was finished, all Israel who were found in the cities of Judah went out and broke down the pillars and hewed down the Asherahs and pulled down the high places and the altars out of all Judah and Benjamin and in Ephraim and Manasseh until they destroyed them all. And all the children of Israel returned to their cities, each man to his own possessions.
Verse_Ch: 31:2 希西家派定祭司和利未人的班次，使祭司和利未人各按班次，各照职任，献燔祭和平安祭，在耶和华殿门内事奉，称谢赞美耶和华。 ###EN### And Hezekiah appointed the divisions of the priests and the Levites by their divisions, each of the priests and the Levites according to his service, for the burnt offering and for the peace offerings, to minister and give thanks and praise in the gates of the temple of Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 31:3 又从王的产业中设定作燔祭的部分，作早晚的燔祭，和安息日、月朔、并节期的燔祭，都是按耶和华律法上所记载的。 ###EN### Also he appointed the king's portion of his substance for the burnt offerings, the morning and evening burnt offerings and the burnt offerings of the Sabbaths and the new moon and the appointed feasts, as written in the law of Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 31:4 他又吩咐住耶路撒冷的百姓将祭司和利未人所应得的分给他们，使他们专心履行耶和华的律法。 ###EN### And he commanded the people who dwelt in Jerusalem to give the portion for the priests and the Levites, that they might devote themselves to the law of Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 31:5 谕旨一出，以色列人就把初熟的五谷、新酒、新油、蜜、和田地各样的出产，多多送来，又把各物的十分之一多多送来。 ###EN### And when the commandment went out, the children of Israel gave in abundance the firstfruits of the grain and of the new wine and of the fresh oil and of the honey and of all the produce of the field; and they brought the tithe of everything in abundance.
Verse_Ch: 31:6 住犹大各城的以色列人和犹大人，也将牛羊的十分之一，并分别为圣归耶和华他们神之圣物中的十分之一，尽都送来，累积成堆。 ###EN### And the children of Israel and Judah who dwelt in the cities of Judah also brought the tithe of the herd and of the flock and the tithe of the dedicated things, which had been dedicated to Jehovah their God, and laid them heap upon heap.
Verse_Ch: 31:7 从三月开始堆积，到七月才完。 ###EN### In the third month they began to lay the foundation of the heaps, and in the seventh month they finished.
Verse_Ch: 31:8 希西家和众首领来，看见堆积的物品，就颂赞耶和华，又为耶和华的民以色列人祝福。 ###EN### And when Hezekiah and the leaders came and saw the heaps, they blessed Jehovah and His people Israel.
Verse_Ch: 31:9 希西家向祭司和利未人查问这堆积的物品； ###EN### And Hezekiah inquired of the priests and the Levites concerning the heaps;
Verse_Ch: 31:10 撒督家的祭司长亚撒利雅对他说，自从民将供物送到耶和华殿以来，我们不但吃饱，且剩下的甚多；因为耶和华赐福给祂的民，所剩下的才这样丰 盛。 ###EN### And Azariah the chief priest, of the house of Zadok, spoke to him and said, Since the time that the people began to bring the contributions into the house of Jehovah, there has been eating and satisfaction and an abundance left over, for Jehovah has blessed His people and this great amount is left over.
Verse_Ch: 31:11 希西家吩咐在耶和华殿里预备仓房，他们就预备了。 ###EN### Then Hezekiah gave orders to prepare storechambers in the house of Jehovah, and they prepared them.
Verse_Ch: 31:12 他们忠信的将供物和十分取一之物，并分别为圣之物，都搬入仓内。利未人歌楠雅掌管这些物品，他兄弟示每为副管， ###EN### And they brought in the contribution and the tithe and dedicated things faithfully. And Conaniah the Levite was ruler over them, and Shimei his brother was second,
Verse_Ch: 31:13 耶歇、亚撒细雅、拿哈、亚撒黑、耶利末、约撒拔、以列、伊斯玛基雅、玛哈、比拿雅都是督理，在歌楠雅和他兄弟示每的手下，是希西家王和管 理神殿的亚撒利雅所派的。 ###EN### And Jehiel and Azaziah and Nahath and Asahel and Jerimoth and Jozabad and Eliel and Ismachiah and Mahath and Benaiah were overseers under Conaniah and Shimei his brother by appointment of Hezekiah the king and Azariah the ruler of the house of God.

Verse_Ch: 31:15 在他手下有伊甸、民雅民、耶书亚、示玛雅、亚玛利雅、示迦尼雅，在祭司的各城里忠信的供职，弟兄无论大小，都按着班次，把他们应得的分， 分给他们。 ###EN### And under him were Eden and Miniamin and Jeshua and Shemaiah, Amariah, and Shecaniah, serving faithfully in the cities of the priests, to distribute the portion to their brothers by divisions, great and small alike,
Verse_Ch: 31:16 但那些按家谱登记，从三岁以上的男丁，凡每日照本分进耶和华的殿供职，按班次尽职守的，不包括在内。 ###EN### Except for those who were enrolled by genealogy, males from three years old and upward, all who went to the house of Jehovah for their day-to-day duties, for their service in their offices by their divisions.
Verse_Ch: 31:17 至于祭司家谱的登记，乃是按宗族；二十岁以上之利未人的登记，乃是按班次中的职守。 ###EN### Now concerning the enrollment of the priests by genealogy, it was by their fathers' houses; and that of the Levites of those twenty years old and upward was by their offices in their divisions.

Verse_Ch: 31:19 至于亚伦子孙作祭司，住在各城郊野的，在每一城都有题名的人，把应得的分，分给祭司中所有的男丁，和一切按家谱登记的利未人。 ###EN### And for the sons of Aaron the priests, who were in the pasture lands of their cities, in each and every city, there were men who were mentioned by name, to distribute portions to all the males among the priests and to all who were enrolled by genealogy among the Levites.
Verse_Ch: 31:20 希西家在犹大遍地这样办理，在耶和华他的神面前行美善、正直、忠信的事。 ###EN### And Hezekiah did this throughout all Judah, and he did what was good and upright and faithful before Jehovah his God.
Verse_Ch: 31:21 凡他着手所办的事，无论是关于神殿的事奉，或是遵照律法和诫命，为要寻求他的神，他都尽心去行，无不亨通。 ###EN### And in every work that he began in service to the house of God and in accordance with the law and the commandment, to seek after his God, he did it with all his heart and prospered.
Chapter_Header: 32

Verse_Ch: 32:16 西拿基立的臣仆还说了别的话，攻击耶和华神和祂的仆人希西家。 ###EN### And his servants said still more against Jehovah God and against Hezekiah His servant.
