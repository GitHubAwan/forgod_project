Verse_Ch: 1:11 他们从那里去攻击底璧的居民。(底璧从前名叫基列西弗。) ###EN### And he went up from there to the inhabitants of Debir. (Now the name of Debir was formerly Kiriath-sepher.)

Verse_Ch: 1:13 迦勒弟弟基纳斯的儿子俄陀聂夺取了那城，迦勒就把女儿押撒给他为妻。 ###EN### Then Othniel the son of Kenaz, Caleb's younger brother, took it; and he gave him Achsah his daughter as wife.
Verse_Ch: 1:14 押撒过门的时候，劝丈夫向她父亲求一块田。押撒一下驴，迦勒问她说，你要什么？ ###EN### And when she came to him, she urged him to ask her father for the field. And she alighted from her donkey, and Caleb said to her, What do you want?

Verse_Ch: 1:36 亚摩利人的境界，是从亚克拉滨坡，从西拉而上。 ###EN### And the border of the Amorites was from the ascent of Akrabbim, from Sela and upward.
Chapter_Header: 2
Verse_Ch: 2:1 耶和华的使者从吉甲上到波金，对以色列人说，我使你们从埃及上来，领你们到我向你们列祖起誓应许之地。我又说，我永不废弃与你们所立的约； ###EN### Then the Angel of Jehovah went up from Gilgal to Bochim. And He said, I caused you to go up out from Egypt, and I brought you into the land that I swore to your fathers. And I said, I will never break My covenant with you,
Verse_Ch: 2:2 你们也不可与这地的居民立约，要拆毁他们的祭坛。你们竟没有听从我的话。你们这作的是什么事？ ###EN### And you shall not make a covenant with the inhabitants of this land; you shall tear down their altars. But you did not listen to My voice. What is this that you have done?
Verse_Ch: 2:3 因此我又说，我必不将他们从你们面前赶出；他们必作你们肋下的荆棘；他们的神必作你们的网罗。 ###EN### Therefore I also said, I will not drive them out from before you; but they will be like thorns in your sides, and their gods will be a snare to you.
Verse_Ch: 2:4 耶和华的使者向以色列众人说这话的时候，百姓就放声而哭。 ###EN### And when the Angel of Jehovah had spoken these words to all the children of Israel, the people lifted up their voice and wept.
Verse_Ch: 2:5 于是给那地方起名叫波金。众人在那里向耶和华献祭。 ###EN### And they called the name of that place Bochim. And they offered to Jehovah there.
Verse_Ch: 2:6 从前约书亚打发百姓去的时候，以色列人各归自己的地业，占据那地。 ###EN### Now when Joshua sent the people away, each of the children of Israel went to his own inheritance to possess the land.
Verse_Ch: 2:7 约书亚在世的日子，以及约书亚死后，那些见过耶和华为以色列人所行大事的长老还在的日子，百姓都事奉耶和华。 ###EN### And the people served Jehovah throughout all the days of Joshua and throughout all the days of the elders whose days extended after Joshua's and who saw all the great work of Jehovah that He had done for Israel.
Verse_Ch: 2:8 耶和华的仆人嫩的儿子约书亚，正一百一十岁就死了。 ###EN### And Joshua the son of Nun, the servant of Jehovah, died at the age of one hundred and ten years.
Verse_Ch: 2:9 以色列人将他葬在他地业的境内，就是在以法莲山地的亭拿希烈，在迦实山的北边。 ###EN### And they buried him in the territory of his inheritance, in Timnath-heres, in the hill country of Ephraim, north of Mount Gaash.
Verse_Ch: 2:10 那一代的人也都归了自己的列祖；在他们之后，有另一代的人兴起，不认识耶和华，也不知道耶和华为以色列人所行的事。 ###EN### And all that generation as well were gathered to their fathers; and another generation, who did not know Jehovah or the work that He had done for Israel, rose up after them.
Verse_Ch: 2:11 以色列人行耶和华眼中看为恶的事，去事奉诸巴力， ###EN### And the children of Israel did what was evil in the sight of Jehovah and served the Baals.
Verse_Ch: 2:12 离弃了领他们出埃及地的耶和华他们列祖的神，去随从跪拜别神，就是四围众民的一些神，惹耶和华发怒； ###EN### And they forsook Jehovah, the God of their fathers, who brought them out from the land of Egypt; and they followed after other gods from among the gods of the peoples who surrounded them; and they worshipped them and provoked Jehovah to anger.
Verse_Ch: 2:13 并离弃耶和华，去事奉巴力和亚斯他录。 ###EN### And they forsook Jehovah and served Baal and the Ashtaroth.
Verse_Ch: 2:14 耶和华的怒气向以色列人发作，把他们交在抢夺者手中，这些人就抢夺他们；祂又将他们交付在四围仇敌的手中，甚至他们在仇敌面前再不能站立得 住。 ###EN### Then the anger of Jehovah burned against Israel, and He delivered them into the hands of plunderers; and they plundered them. And He sold them into the hand of their enemies around them, and they were no longer able to stand before their enemies.
Verse_Ch: 2:15 他们无论往何处去，耶和华的手都以灾祸攻击他们，正如耶和华所说的，又如耶和华向他们所起誓的；他们便极其困苦。 ###EN### Whenever they went out, the hand of Jehovah was against them for evil, as Jehovah had spoken and as Jehovah had sworn to them; and they were greatly distressed.
Verse_Ch: 2:16 耶和华兴起士师，士师就拯救他们脱离抢夺他们之人的手。 ###EN### And Jehovah raised up judges, and they saved them out of the hands of those who plundered them.
Verse_Ch: 2:17 但他们也不听从士师，竟随从别神，行了邪淫，向这些神跪拜，速速的偏离他们列祖所行的道路，不像他们列祖那样听从耶和华的命令。 ###EN### Yet they did not listen to their judges either, but they went about as harlots after other gods and worshipped them. Verse_Ch: 2:18 耶和华为他们兴起士师，就与那士师同在。那士师在世的一切日子，耶和华拯救他们脱离仇敌的手；他们因受人压迫欺凌，就哀声叹气，耶和华便怜 恤他们。 ###EN### And when Jehovah raised up judges for them, Jehovah was with the judge, and He saved them from the hand of their enemies throughout all the days of the judge; for Jehovah would be moved to pity by their groaning because of those who oppressed them and crushed them.

Verse_Ch: 2:20 于是耶和华的怒气向以色列人发作；祂说，因这民违背我所吩咐他们列祖遵守的约，不听从我的话， ###EN### And the anger of Jehovah burned against Israel; and He said, Because this nation has transgressed My covenant which I commanded their fathers and they do not listen to My voice,
Verse_Ch: 2:21 所以约书亚死的时候所剩下的各族，我也必不再从他们面前赶出， ###EN### I for My part will no longer dispossess from before them any of the nations that Joshua left when he died,
Verse_Ch: 2:22 为要借着这剩下的各族，试验以色列人，看他们肯不肯谨守遵行我的道路，像他们列祖谨守遵行一样。 ###EN### In order to test Israel through them as to whether or not they will keep the way of Jehovah by walking in it, as their fathers kept it.
Verse_Ch: 2:23 这样，耶和华留下各族，不将他们速速赶出，也没有交在约书亚的手中。 ###EN### So Jehovah left these nations, not dispossessing them quickly, and did not deliver them into the hand of Joshua.

Verse_Ch: 3:3 所留下的就是非利士人的五个首领，和一切迦南人，西顿人，并住利巴嫩山，从巴力黑们山直到哈马口的希未人。 ###EN### The five lords of the Philistines and all the Canaanites and the Sidonians and the Hivites who dwelt on Mount Lebanon, from Mount Baal-hermon to the entrance of Hamath.

Verse_Ch: 3:9 以色列人哀求耶和华，耶和华就为他们兴起一位拯救者救他们，就是迦勒弟弟基纳斯的儿子俄陀聂。 ###EN### Then the children of Israel cried out to Jehovah, and Jehovah raised up a savior for the children of Israel, who saved them, that is, Othniel the son of Kenaz, Caleb's younger brother.

Verse_Ch: 3:11 于是那地太平四十年。基纳斯的儿子俄陀聂死了。 ###EN### And the land had rest forty years. And Othniel the son of Kenaz died.
Verse_Ch: 3:12 以色列人又行耶和华眼中看为恶的事；耶和华就使摩押王伊矶伦强盛，攻击以色列人，因为他们行耶和华眼中看为恶的事。 ###EN### Then the children of Israel again did that which was evil in the sight of Jehovah. And Jehovah strengthened Eglon the king of Moab against Israel because they did that which was evil in the sight of Jehovah.
Verse_Ch: 3:13 伊矶伦招聚亚扪人和亚玛力人，前去攻击以色列，占据棕树城。 ###EN### And he gathered the children of Ammon and Amalek to himself and went and struck Israel, and they took possession of the city of palms.

Verse_Ch: 3:15 以色列人哀求耶和华，耶和华就为他们兴起一位拯救者，就是便雅悯人基拉的儿子以笏；他是惯用左手的。以色列人托他送贡物给摩押王伊矶伦。 ###EN### Then the children of Israel cried out to Jehovah, and Jehovah raised up a savior for them, that is, Ehud the son of Gera, the Benjaminite, a left-handed man. Now the children of Israel sent tribute to Eglon the king of Moab by him.
