关于 WordsGod.com

Welcome to WordsGod.com — a space created by followers of Christ to share the living Word of God with simplicity, reverence, and consistency. We believe that God's Word is powerful, alive, and life-giving. Here, you’ll find daily readings, spiritual reflections, and quiet moments with the Lord — all in just a few minutes a day. May His Word dwell in you richly.

WordsGod.com exists to help believers connect with God through His Word. In a noisy and distracted world, we offer a quiet corner to hear what the Spirit is saying — day by day, verse by verse. We are not a brand or a business, but simply brothers and sisters in Christ desiring to share what we’ve received.

This is a place for hearts that hunger for God. Whether you are new to the Bible or seasoned in the faith, you’ll find fresh encouragement, daily Scripture, and grace-filled truth drawn from God's Word. No ads. No distractions. Only the Word.

About Me

My name is Awan, a simple brother in Christ. I’m not a pastor or a theologian — just a believer who has personally experienced the power and peace that come from reading God’s Word daily.

Over time, I became burdened that many Christian apps are cluttered with ads or distractions that pull our hearts away from Christ. I felt led to build a clean, reverent, and simple space where anyone — no matter how busy — could come and spend ten quiet minutes with God every day.

WordsGod.com is my small offering to the Lord. May it serve you and bring you closer to Him.

In Christ,
Awan

关于我

我是张弟兄，是一位普通的基督徒。不是牧师，也不是传道人，只是一位在主的话语中尝过滋味、深知主恩典的弟兄。

WordsGod.com 是我献给主的一点小小心意。愿主使用这个平台，帮助你天天亲近祂。

在主里，
